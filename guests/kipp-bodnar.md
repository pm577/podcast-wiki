# Kipp Bodnar

**Role:** Chief Marketing Officer at HubSpot

**Key Beliefs:**
- The best marketers think like VCs: they build a portfolio of bets, test multiple channels, and double down on what works. (confidence: 0.9)
- When testing new channels, allocate a small budget (e.g., 5-10% of total) and set clear success metrics to decide whether to scale or stop. (confidence: 0.9)
- Know when to stop a channel: if it doesn't meet your target ROI or growth efficiency within a defined timeframe, cut it. (confidence: 0.9)
- Don't start with product marketing; start with understanding the customer's problem and then build the product and messaging around it. (confidence: 0.8)
- Hire for adaptability and a growth mindset rather than specific channel expertise, because channels change rapidly. (confidence: 0.8)
