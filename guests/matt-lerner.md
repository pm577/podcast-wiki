# Matt Lerner

**Key Beliefs:**
- Run A/B tests on small changes to your landing page or signup flow to improve conversion rates. (confidence: 0.9)
- Leverage social proof and testimonials on your website to build trust and increase conversions. (confidence: 0.9)
- Focus on one metric at a time to drive growth, rather than trying to improve everything at once. (confidence: 0.8)
- Use cohort analysis to understand user behavior over time and identify where users drop off. (confidence: 0.8)
- Identify your 'aha moment' – the point where users realize the value of your product – and optimize the onboarding to get users there faster. (confidence: 0.8)
