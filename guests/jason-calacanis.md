# Jason Calacanis

**Key Beliefs:**
- Founders should be obsessed with product-market fit and ignore everything else until they achieve it. (confidence: 0.95)
- Investors must ask founders four key questions: 1) Why this? 2) Why now? 3) Why you? 4) Why not? (confidence: 0.9)
- Early founder liquidity is good, not bad, because it allows founders to focus on the business without personal financial stress. (confidence: 0.9)
- Investors should look for founders who are 'unfairly advantaged' in their market, such as deep domain expertise or unique insights. (confidence: 0.85)
- YC has scaled successfully because they maintain a high bar for selection and provide a structured program that works even with larger cohorts. (confidence: 0.8)
