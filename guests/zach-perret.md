# Zach Perret

**Key Beliefs:**
- You have to hire for spikes. You want people who are world-class at one thing, not generalists. (confidence: 0.9)
- The two big questions CEOs should ask themselves: 1) What is the one thing that must be true for our business to succeed? 2) What is the one thing that could kill our business? (confidence: 0.9)
- When hiring, look for people who have demonstrated excellence in a specific area, not just broad experience. (confidence: 0.9)
- You need to be comfortable with being uncomfortable. The best founders are those who can handle ambiguity and uncertainty. (confidence: 0.8)
- Fintech predictions: incumbent entrants will become more aggressive, and Europe will rise as a fintech hub. (confidence: 0.7)
