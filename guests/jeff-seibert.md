# Jeff Seibert

**Key Beliefs:**
- When hiring remotely, focus on communication skills and self-motivation because you can't walk over to someone's desk. (confidence: 0.8)
- Capital efficiency is key: focus on unit economics and runway extension, especially in uncertain times. (confidence: 0.8)
- Use a structured interview process with consistent questions to reduce bias and improve hiring quality. (confidence: 0.7)
- Raise money before you need it, and from investors who add strategic value beyond capital. (confidence: 0.7)
- Pre-launch, focus on building a product that solves a real pain point for a specific user segment. (confidence: 0.6)
