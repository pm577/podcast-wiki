# Dennis Mortensen

**Key Beliefs:**
- Product-market fit is like porn: you know it when you see it, but it's hard to define. (confidence: 0.9)
- AI startups should focus on solving a single, clear problem rather than trying to be a general assistant. (confidence: 0.9)
- The entrance of Facebook and Google into enterprise software forces traditional enterprise companies to innovate faster or risk being disrupted. (confidence: 0.8)
- Hiring for culture fit is overrated; hire for ability to execute and adapt. (confidence: 0.8)
- Future pricing model for enterprise software will shift from per-seat licensing to value-based pricing. (confidence: 0.7)
