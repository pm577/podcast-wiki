# Mo Koyfman

**Role:** Founder and General Partner at Shine Capital

**Key Beliefs:**
- The secret to winning in venture is splitting deals, i.e., co-investing with other funds to diversify risk and access better deal flow. (confidence: 0.9)
- Anyone that always does their pro-rata is wrong; you should be selective about which companies to double down on. (confidence: 0.9)
- Small funds outperform large funds because they can invest in smaller, earlier-stage companies and have more flexibility. (confidence: 0.8)
- Invest in founders who have deep domain expertise and a clear vision. (confidence: 0.8)
- Learn from biggest hits and biggest losses to refine your investment thesis. (confidence: 0.7)
