# Matteo Franceschetti

**Key Beliefs:**
- The five questions to ask every new hire: (1) What do you want to be famous for? (2) What is the biggest misconception people have about you? (3) What is the hardest feedback you've ever received? (4) What is the hardest feedback you've ever given? (5) What do you do for fun? (confidence: 0.95)
- Great leaders give feedback immediately, in private, and focus on behavior not personality. (confidence: 0.9)
- During a hard fundraise, be transparent about challenges and focus on the long-term vision; investors appreciate honesty and resilience. (confidence: 0.9)
- VCs add value by providing strategic advice, introductions, and support during tough times, but not all VCs are equal; founders should choose investors who have domain expertise and a track record of helping portfolio companies. (confidence: 0.85)
- To solve sleep, you need to understand the science of sleep and build a product that is both effective and easy to use; it's a hard problem that requires decades of commitment. (confidence: 0.8)
