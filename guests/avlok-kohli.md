# Avlok Kohli

**Role:** CEO at AngelList Venture

**Key Beliefs:**

- Defensibility is BS on day 1; you need to focus on distribution and product-market fit first. (confidence: 0.9)
- Market is more important than founder; a great market can carry a mediocre team, but a great team cannot overcome a bad market. (confidence: 0.9)
- Being first to market doesn't matter; what matters is being the best at distribution and execution. (confidence: 0.9)
- Founders should not optimize for valuation at seed; they should optimize for the right investors and terms that set them up for future rounds. (confidence: 0.9)
