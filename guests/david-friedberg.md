# David Friedberg

**Role:** Founder and CEO at The Production Board (TPB)

**Key Beliefs:**
- Interest rate hikes impact venture capital by making risk-free assets more attractive, thus reducing the appetite for risky investments like startups. (confidence: 0.9)
- When incubating a company, start with a clear thesis about the problem and the solution, then build a team around that thesis rather than the other way around. (confidence: 0.9)
- The bifurcation in venture today is between companies that are capital-efficient and those that are capital-intensive, and the market is rewarding capital efficiency more now. (confidence: 0.8)
- To create business value, focus on building a moat through technology or network effects that makes it difficult for competitors to replicate. (confidence: 0.8)
- Founders should be willing to pivot based on data and customer feedback, but the core thesis should remain intact. (confidence: 0.7)
