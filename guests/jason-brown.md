# Jason Brown

**Key Beliefs:**
- To determine if an investor is truly aligned to your mission, look for those who have a long-term perspective and are willing to support you through ups and downs. (confidence: 0.8)
- The two traits that make truly special board members are intellectual honesty and the ability to provide strategic guidance without being operational. (confidence: 0.8)
- Startup valuations are not as overpriced as you think because the market is efficient and pricing reflects the potential for outsized returns. (confidence: 0.7)
