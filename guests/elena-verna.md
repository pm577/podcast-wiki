# Elena Verna

**Key Beliefs:**
- Building in public coupled with employee and founder-led socials is a key growth strategy. (confidence: 0.95)
- Give your product away freely to remove barriers; if users ask for free credits for hackathons, give them what they need to create word-of-mouth. (confidence: 0.95)
- Only 30-40% of traditional growth playbook transfers to AI companies; need to spend 95% on innovating growth loops and only 5% on optimization. (confidence: 0.9)
- The only way to create a word-of-mouth loop is to blow users' socks off with the product experience. (confidence: 0.9)
- Ship things you can talk about to drive growth; focus on building features that are newsworthy. (confidence: 0.9)
