# Christian Lanng

**Key Beliefs:**
- Founders need to recognize burnout signs early: loss of passion, physical symptoms, and inability to disconnect. (confidence: 0.9)
- Seat pricing is over; pricing should be based on value delivered, not per user. (confidence: 0.9)
- Founders should not lie to themselves about the difficulty of the journey; it's okay to step away. (confidence: 0.9)
- AI co-pilot is BS; AI should be autonomous agents that complete tasks without human intervention. (confidence: 0.85)
- Venture capital can be toxic; founders must prioritize mental health over fundraising. (confidence: 0.85)
