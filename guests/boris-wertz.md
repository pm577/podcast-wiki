# Boris Wertz

**Key Beliefs:**
- Marketplaces need to solve a liquidity problem first, not a technology problem. (confidence: 0.9)
- The most successful marketplaces start with a very narrow vertical or geography to achieve density. (confidence: 0.9)
- Marketplaces should focus on the supply side first, then demand. (confidence: 0.9)
- For marketplaces, the key metric is liquidity, not just growth. (confidence: 0.85)
- Marketplaces need to have a high degree of trust and safety mechanisms to scale. (confidence: 0.85)
