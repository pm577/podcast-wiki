# Ralf Wenzel

**Key Beliefs:**
- Quick commerce is about delivering groceries and daily essentials in 15 minutes or less, powered by a network of dark stores located close to customers. (confidence: 0.9)
- The five core components consumers care about when ordering today are: speed, selection, price, quality, and service. (confidence: 0.9)
- To succeed in quick commerce, you need to optimize for unit economics by balancing basket size, delivery density, and order frequency. (confidence: 0.9)
- The key to winning in quick commerce is to have a relentless focus on the customer experience, especially speed and reliability. (confidence: 0.9)
- CPGs are misaligned because they are powered by ad spend, which incentivizes them to push products regardless of consumer demand, leading to inefficiencies. (confidence: 0.8)
