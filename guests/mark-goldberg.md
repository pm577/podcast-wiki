# Mark Goldberg

**Key Beliefs:**
- Founders must ask multi-stage funds about their investment strategy for later rounds, specifically whether they lead or follow, and how they handle pro rata. (confidence: 0.9)
- Founders should evaluate whether a multi-stage fund has the ability and willingness to support them through multiple rounds, not just the seed. (confidence: 0.9)
- Most angels will lose their money because they lack the ability to provide follow-on capital and access to later-stage rounds. (confidence: 0.8)
- The key to success in venture capital is having a differentiated thesis and being able to add value beyond capital. (confidence: 0.8)
- We will see the first $100 billion neo-bank shortly, driven by the shift from traditional banking to digital-first financial services. (confidence: 0.7)
