# Ben Horowitz

**Key Beliefs:**
- The worst thing that you do as a leader is you hesitate on the next decision. The thing that causes you to hesitate is both decisions are horrible. (confidence: 1.0)
- The only value you ever add is when you make a decision that most people don't like. (confidence: 1.0)
- Success is a bunch of little things. It's a small thing, a small thing that's hard to do that doesn't seem to have a high impact, but it leads to the next small hard to do thing and then eventually you get an outcome. (confidence: 1.0)
- All the things that you perceive that are happening to you that are bad... is very small compared to what you do to yourself. If you believe what people say about you, if you believe what they did to you, then that destroys you. But if you go, 'That's not me,' you can overcome almost anything. (confidence: 1.0)
