# Matt Mazzeo

**Role:** Managing Director at Lowercase Capital

**Key Beliefs:**
- The three KPIs for successful investing are: 1) access to the best deals, 2) ability to add value to portfolio companies, and 3) generating strong returns. (confidence: 0.8)
- To get access to the best deals, you need to build a strong personal brand and network so that entrepreneurs come to you first. (confidence: 0.8)
- Adding value to portfolio companies means helping with hiring, product strategy, introductions, and being a sounding board for founders. (confidence: 0.8)
- As a founder, you should seek investors who have a clear thesis and can articulate how they will help you beyond capital. (confidence: 0.8)
- Generating strong returns requires discipline in valuation and knowing when to say no to a deal, even if it's popular. (confidence: 0.7)
