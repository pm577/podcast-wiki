# Scott Farquhar

**Role:** Co-Founder & Co-CEO at Atlassian

**Key Beliefs:**
- The four roles of the CEO are: (1) setting the vision and strategy, (2) hiring and building the team, (3) communicating effectively, and (4) ensuring execution and accountability. (confidence: 0.9)
- Regrets of omission (not doing something) are worse than regrets of commission (doing something wrong) because you can learn from mistakes but you never know what you missed. (confidence: 0.9)
- Scaling a company requires a shift from being a doer to a leader who enables others, which is a difficult transition for many founders. (confidence: 0.9)
- When raising funding, it's important to choose investors who will be long-term partners and not just provide capital, as seen with Accel's investment that returned $6BN. (confidence: 0.85)
- The honeymoon period for a new CEO or leader is short; you need to quickly establish credibility and deliver results. (confidence: 0.8)
