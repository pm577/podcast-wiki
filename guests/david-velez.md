# David Velez

**Key Beliefs:**
- Focus on a single product and do it exceptionally well before expanding. Nubank started with a no-fee credit card and built trust before adding other products. (confidence: 0.9)
- Use customer obsession as a competitive advantage. Nubank's NPS is extremely high because they relentlessly focus on customer experience and simplicity. (confidence: 0.9)
- In emerging markets, start with a product that solves a clear pain point (e.g., high credit card fees) and leverage word-of-mouth for growth. (confidence: 0.9)
- When expanding internationally, adapt to local regulations and customer behaviors rather than copying the Brazilian model exactly. (confidence: 0.9)
- Hire for mission alignment and cultural fit over pure skill. Nubank prioritizes people who believe in the mission of fighting complexity and empowering people. (confidence: 0.8)
