# Jason Lemkin

**Key Beliefs:**
- Hire your first salesperson when you have closed the first 10 customers and are spending more than 20% of your time on sales. (confidence: 0.95)
- Prioritize the early success of sales reps by allowing them to keep 100% of their initial sales for the first three months. (confidence: 0.95)
- Initiate a weekly meeting between the VP of Sales and the VP of Product to discuss the budget allocation for feature requests and prioritize it. (confidence: 0.95)
- Ensure that there are clear next steps after every meeting, even if it’s doing a demo for someone else in the company. The best salespeople don’t throw a Hail Mary—they advance the ball into the red zone. (confidence: 0.95)
- Don’t be swayed solely by impressive resumes or acronyms; instead, seek out those individuals who you would personally buy your product from. (confidence: 0.9)
