# Ann Miura Ko

**Role:** Co-Founding Partner at Floodgate

**Key Beliefs:**
- The four required powers for a startup to be a $500m business are: 1) Insight Power - a unique insight into a problem, 2) Product Power - a product that delivers 10x better solution, 3) Team Power - a team that can execute, and 4) Market Power - a large and growing market. (confidence: 0.9)
- Product Power requires a product that is 10x better than existing solutions, not just incrementally better. (confidence: 0.9)
- Insight Power means having a unique insight into a problem that others don't see, often from personal experience or deep domain knowledge. (confidence: 0.85)
- Team Power means having a team that can execute and adapt, with complementary skills and a shared vision. (confidence: 0.85)
- Market Power requires a large and growing market, ideally with a TAM of at least $1B. (confidence: 0.8)
