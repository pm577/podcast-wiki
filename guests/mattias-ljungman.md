# Mattias Ljungman

**Key Beliefs:**
- A truly great interview is one where the interviewer listens more than they speak and asks follow-up questions that dig deeper. (confidence: 0.9)
- The best way to learn about venture capital is to listen to a wide range of investors and synthesize their perspectives. (confidence: 0.9)
- Founders should focus on building a product that solves a real problem rather than chasing trends. (confidence: 0.85)
- The best investors are those who have a deep understanding of the founder's psychology and can empathize with the journey. (confidence: 0.8)
- Investors should look for founders who are resilient and have a clear vision, even if the initial pitch is rough. (confidence: 0.75)
