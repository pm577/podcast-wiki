# Jeremy Henrickson

**Role:** Senior Vice President of Product at Rippling

**Key Beliefs:**
- It's very, very tempting to float up here as a leader and say, 'Hey, you take that hill over there. You guys do this over here.' When in fact, where you really learn where the challenges are, or the problems or the successes is by just being there with the people in the trenches on one of the things, whichever one seems hardest or most complicated. (confidence: 1.0)
- You want small teams with clear missions. (confidence: 1.0)
- The more you can bake into a clear platform, it reduces the decision-making complexity for everyone who's working on the domain part of the problem. (confidence: 0.9)
