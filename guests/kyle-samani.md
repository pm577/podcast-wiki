# Kyle Samani

**Key Beliefs:**
- Market matters more than founding team. A great team in a bad market will fail, but a mediocre team in a great market can succeed. (confidence: 0.9)
- Bitcoin is not a hedge against inflation because it is correlated with risk assets and has high volatility. (confidence: 0.9)
- Crypto investing is less collaborative than traditional VC because of the competitive nature and lack of information sharing. (confidence: 0.8)
- Network effects in crypto are different: they are based on liquidity and developer activity, not just user base. (confidence: 0.8)
- Solana will beat Ethereum because of its superior scalability and lower transaction costs, enabling more applications. (confidence: 0.7)
