# Sarah Guo

**Key Beliefs:**
- Defensibility in startups is BS; speed is everything. The best defense is to keep moving fast and stay ahead. (confidence: 0.95)
- In AI, startups have an advantage over incumbents because they can move faster and are not constrained by legacy systems or existing business models. (confidence: 0.9)
- Investors should focus on backing founders who prioritize speed and adaptability over traditional defensibility moats. (confidence: 0.9)
- Large groups worsen decision-making; smaller teams (e.g., 10 people) can build $10BN+ companies because they are more agile and efficient. (confidence: 0.85)
- Wealth inequality will increase as AI enables small teams to capture disproportionate value, but this also creates opportunities for new wealth creation. (confidence: 0.8)
