# Hussein Kanji

**Key Beliefs:**
- Fundraise for a set amount of time, not to achieve a certain amount, to avoid wasting time and momentum. (confidence: 0.9)
- Large seed rounds increase the chances of success because they allow founders to focus on building the product and acquiring customers without constant fundraising pressure. (confidence: 0.8)
- When to sell in venture: sell when you have a clear offer that provides a good return, and don't hold out for a higher price that may never come. (confidence: 0.8)
- AI means London can compete with the US because AI reduces the importance of scale and network effects, allowing smaller ecosystems to thrive. (confidence: 0.8)
- Multi-stage firms do not do the work because they rely on brand and scale rather than deep due diligence and hands-on support. (confidence: 0.7)
