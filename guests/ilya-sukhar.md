# Ilya Sukhar

**Role:** General Partner at Matrix Partners

**Key Beliefs:**

- Effective referencing in fundraising involves getting warm introductions from trusted mutual connections, not just cold emails or LinkedIn messages. (confidence: 0.9)
- The seed ecosystem could be optimized by having more specialized seed funds that provide hands-on support and domain expertise, rather than just capital. (confidence: 0.8)
- Founders should focus on building a product that solves a real pain point for a specific user segment, rather than trying to appeal to everyone. (confidence: 0.8)
