# Daniel Yanisse

**Role:** Co-founder and CEO at Checkr

**Key Beliefs:**
- The biggest mistake founders make in hiring is not being clear about what they need and moving too fast without a structured process. (confidence: 0.9)
- Profitability gives you leverage in fundraising because you can walk away from bad terms. (confidence: 0.9)
- When moving upmarket, hire experienced enterprise sales reps early to build the playbook. (confidence: 0.9)
- When fundraising, optimize for the right investors who add value beyond capital, not just the highest valuation. (confidence: 0.85)
- Moving from SMB to enterprise requires a shift in sales motion, product features, and customer support; doing it too early can dilute focus. (confidence: 0.85)
