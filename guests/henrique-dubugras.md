# Henrique Dubugras

**Role:** Founder & CEO at Brex

**Key Beliefs:**
- The biggest hiring mistake when scaling is hiring people who are too similar to you or your existing team, because you need diversity of thought and experience. (confidence: 0.9)
- Product messaging should focus on the problem you solve, not the features you have. Most companies get this wrong by leading with features. (confidence: 0.9)
- As a CEO, you should spend time on the things only you can do, and delegate everything else. Advice from Evan Spiegel and Eric Schmidt. (confidence: 0.9)
- When launching secondary products, the right time is when you have a clear distribution advantage or a unique insight that your competitors don't have. (confidence: 0.85)
- When hiring, look for people who have a growth mindset and are adaptable, because the company will change rapidly. (confidence: 0.85)
