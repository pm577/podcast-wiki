# Beezer Clarkson

**Key Beliefs:**
- LPs are open for business but they are being more selective and doing more due diligence. (confidence: 0.9)
- Funds need to show a clear differentiation and a strong track record to raise capital today. (confidence: 0.9)
- Funds that are not top-quartile performers will be hit hardest in the current fundraising environment. (confidence: 0.9)
- LPs want to see more alignment of interests, such as GPs having more skin in the game. (confidence: 0.85)
- Emerging managers need to build relationships with LPs early and often, not just when raising a fund. (confidence: 0.85)
