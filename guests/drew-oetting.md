# Drew Oetting

**Role:** Founding Partner at 8VC

**Key Beliefs:**
- VCs should invest in entrepreneurs, not technologists. The key is to back people who can build and lead a company, not just those with technical skills. (confidence: 0.8)
- Growth creates value when it is sustainable and unit economics are positive; growth destroys value when it is fueled by unsustainable spending or negative unit economics. (confidence: 0.8)
- To bootstrap learning that most people acquire over decades, you should surround yourself with smart people, read voraciously, and seek out mentors who can compress your learning curve. (confidence: 0.7)
