# Tal Raviv

**Role:** Individual Contributor (IC) Product Manager at Previously at Patreon, Riverside, Wix, and AppsFlyer

**Key Beliefs:**
- Product management is unfair. We’re expected to be on both a maker and a manager’s schedule. (confidence: 1.0)
- Fairness isn’t coming. (confidence: 1.0)
- PMs who thrive will be the ones who build their own systems, methodologies, and tools to make the job work for them, not the other way around. (confidence: 1.0)
- Instead of adding action items from meetings to a to-do list, do the action items live in the meeting. (confidence: 1.0)
- Half of those meetings can be pivoted into an asynchronous communication. The other half can be fast-forwarded by setting up attendees to enter the meeting with context and prepared thoughts. (confidence: 1.0)
- Don’t go into Slack for the first half of your day. (confidence: 1.0)
