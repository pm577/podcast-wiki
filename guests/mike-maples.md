# Mike Maples

**Key Beliefs:**
- Product-market fit is a dance, not a destination; it's about iterating and finding the right market for your product. (confidence: 0.9)
- Category kings are created by founders who have a unique insight about a future that is different from the present, and they are willing to be contrarian and right. (confidence: 0.8)
- Founders should focus on building a product that is 10x better than the existing solution, not just incrementally better. (confidence: 0.8)
- Most venture funds make a mistake when hiring by focusing too much on pedigree and not enough on pattern recognition and the ability to identify contrarian insights. (confidence: 0.7)
- The best companies are often started by founders who are not trying to start a company but are trying to solve a problem they are passionate about. (confidence: 0.7)
