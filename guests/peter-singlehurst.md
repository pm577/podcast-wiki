# Peter Singlehurst

**Key Beliefs:**
- The distinction between public and private markets is BS; the same fundamental analysis applies to both. (confidence: 0.9)
- Portfolio construction should focus on high concentration in the best ideas rather than diversification for its own sake. (confidence: 0.9)
- Loss rates are inevitable; investors should accept that many investments will fail and focus on the winners. (confidence: 0.9)
- To achieve outsized returns, investors must be willing to hold concentrated positions in their highest-conviction bets. (confidence: 0.9)
- There is a misalignment between GPs and LPs in venture capital; GPs often prioritize fund size over returns. (confidence: 0.8)
