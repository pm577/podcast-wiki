# Mike Volpi

**Role:** General Partner at Index Ventures

**Key Beliefs:**
- The transition from operator to investor is like moving from player to coach; you have to learn to derive satisfaction from others' success rather than your own direct impact. (confidence: 0.9)
- When evaluating startups, look for founders who have a deep understanding of their customers' pain points and a unique insight that others have missed. (confidence: 0.9)
- To build a generation-defining brand, you need a product that is not just incrementally better but 10x better than existing solutions, and you must have a clear vision of how the world will change. (confidence: 0.85)
- Enterprise SaaS companies should focus on solving a specific, high-value problem for a well-defined customer segment rather than trying to be everything to everyone. (confidence: 0.85)
- European startups often have a more global mindset from day one compared to US startups, which can be an advantage in building international businesses. (confidence: 0.8)
