# Lulu Cheng Meservey

**Role:** Executive Vice President of Corporate Affairs and Chief Communications Officer at Activision Blizzard

**Key Beliefs:**
- I often say to find your audience's cultural erogenous zones. So what it means is people have things that they either care about or don't, and you're not going to change that. So it's a huge lift to try to change someone's worldview or their passions. It's a light lift to take the thing you want to talk about and just shape it into, to fit into their worldview or their passions. (confidence: 0.95)
- With messaging, it's not build it and they will come. It is so hard and you'd have to be superhumanly gifted to the extent that I can't recall seeing in my entire life, where you create a message and a story so powerful that someone who didn't care at all before suddenly makes that their passion. (confidence: 0.95)
- You want to make it something that a second-grader could understand. You want to minimize the cognitive burden on the recipient. (confidence: 0.9)
