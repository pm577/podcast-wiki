# Jim Swartz

**Role:** Co-founder at Accel

**Key Beliefs:**
- You should invest in markets that are large and growing, and where the founder has a unique insight. (confidence: 0.9)
- Founders should focus on building a product that is 10x better than the competition, not just incrementally better. (confidence: 0.9)
- Hiring is the most important thing a founder does; you need to hire people who are smarter than you and who share your values. (confidence: 0.9)
- Accel will never hire a professional manager as CEO; the firm is built on partnership and trust. (confidence: 0.9)
- The most important thing in venture capital is to back people who are resilient and have a high tolerance for failure. (confidence: 0.8)
