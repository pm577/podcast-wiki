# Ben Rubin

**Role:** Founder & CEO at /talk

**Key Beliefs:**
- When scaling a team, communication breaks down; you need to move from synchronous to asynchronous communication to maintain velocity. (confidence: 0.9)
- Hiring for culture add rather than culture fit is crucial for scaling; you want people who bring new perspectives, not just those who fit in. (confidence: 0.85)
- Founders should focus on building a product that solves a real problem for a specific user group rather than trying to appeal to everyone. (confidence: 0.85)
- Traditional metrics like DAU/MAU are insufficient to determine product success; you need to measure 'time well spent' and user satisfaction. (confidence: 0.8)
- Product-market fit is not a single event; it's a continuous process of iterating based on user feedback and changing market conditions. (confidence: 0.8)
