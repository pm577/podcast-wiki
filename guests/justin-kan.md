# Justin Kan

**Role:** Partner at Y Combinator

**Key Beliefs:**
- Quitting alcohol dramatically improves sleep quality and mental clarity, which are critical for high-performance decision-making. (confidence: 0.9)
- Alcohol negatively impacts REM sleep, leading to reduced cognitive performance the next day. (confidence: 0.9)
- Social pressure to drink is a major obstacle; founders should proactively communicate their choice to avoid awkwardness. (confidence: 0.85)
- Many founders use alcohol as a crutch for stress; addressing root causes of stress is more effective than drinking. (confidence: 0.85)
- Replacing drinking with other rituals (e.g., exercise, meditation) helps maintain social connections without alcohol. (confidence: 0.8)
