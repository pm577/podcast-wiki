# Anna Khan

**Role:** General Partner at CRV

**Key Beliefs:**
- Founders should spend time with analysts when raising because they often have the most time to do deep research and can become strong advocates. (confidence: 0.9)
- To know when to engage vs wait, founders should assess the level of interest from multiple VCs and create a competitive process to drive better terms. (confidence: 0.9)
- Pre-empting rounds are rising because VCs want to secure deals quickly, but founders should be cautious and ensure they are not leaving money on the table. (confidence: 0.85)
- Founders should prioritize building relationships with analysts as they can influence investment decisions and provide valuable feedback. (confidence: 0.85)
- Few firms are doing true early stage investing today because many have moved to later stages or require more traction, creating an opportunity for founders to target the right firms. (confidence: 0.8)
