# Ali Rowghani

**Role:** Partner at Y Combinator

**Key Beliefs:**
- When scaling a company, the most important thing is to hire people who are better than you and give them real responsibility. (confidence: 0.9)
- The best way to build a great culture is to hire people who already embody the values you want, rather than trying to change people after they join. (confidence: 0.9)
- The most important metric for a startup is revenue growth, but you need to make sure it's sustainable and not just driven by spending money on ads. (confidence: 0.9)
- The three traits all truly successful leaders share are: 1) They have a clear vision and can articulate it compellingly, 2) They have high standards and hold people accountable, 3) They are great at building teams and developing people. (confidence: 0.8)
- As a leader, you need to be willing to make decisions that are unpopular in the short term but right for the long term. (confidence: 0.8)
