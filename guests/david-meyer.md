# David Meyer

**Role:** SVP Products at Databricks

- You should not go into product management if you want to be the CEO; the CEO is always the CPO. (confidence: 0.8)
- Founders should focus on product strategy themselves rather than delegating to a product manager. (confidence: 0.8)
- Hire people who aren't in product already to build the best product teams. (confidence: 0.7)
