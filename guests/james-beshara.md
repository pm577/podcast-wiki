# James Beshara

**Role:** Co-Founder at Tilt

**Key Beliefs:**
- The best way to find a co-founder is to work with someone you already know and trust, rather than trying to recruit a stranger. (confidence: 0.8)
- Mobile-first design is critical for crowdfunding because people are more likely to contribute spontaneously when they can do it from their phone. (confidence: 0.8)
- For a marketplace to work, you need to focus on one side of the market first and make that side incredibly valuable before adding the other side. (confidence: 0.7)
- You should raise money from investors who have a thesis that aligns with your business, not just those who write the biggest checks. (confidence: 0.7)
- The key to viral growth is to make the act of sharing a natural part of the user experience, not an afterthought. (confidence: 0.7)
