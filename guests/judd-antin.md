# Judd Antin

**Role:** Consultant, former head of research at Airbnb and Meta at Self-employed

**Key Beliefs:**
- User-centered performance refers to customer obsession or user-centered practice that is symbolic rather than focused on learning. It's hugely common, I would argue. It's work we do to signal to each other how customer obsessed we are, not because we want to make a different decision. (confidence: 0.95)
- We don't validate, we falsify. We are looking to be wrong. (confidence: 0.9)
- The user research discipline over the last 15 years is dying. The reckoning is here. The discipline can still survive and thrive, but we'd better adapt, and quick. (confidence: 0.95)
- Researchers need to be more profit focused. (confidence: 0.85)
