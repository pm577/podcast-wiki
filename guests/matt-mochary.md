# Matt Mochary

**Role:** Coach to venture capitalists and founders at Self-employed

**Key Beliefs:**
- The biggest marker between a botched layoff and a successful layoff is that the person hears it from their manager in a one-on-one, not in an email, group chat, or any group setting. (confidence: 0.95)
- When someone is in fear, their brain makes exaggerated predictions and gives bad advice; the opposite of what fear predicts often happens. (confidence: 0.9)
- CEOs often hide problems from their board out of fear, but this is counterproductive because investors need to know to participate in future rounds. (confidence: 0.9)
- To overcome fear, make a low-stakes bet with someone not in fear: predict what will happen if you act despite fear, and let them predict the opposite; the winner determines future actions. (confidence: 0.85)
