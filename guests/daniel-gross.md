# Daniel Gross

**Key Beliefs:**
- Games are a powerful tool to understand human motivation because they reveal what people do when they have freedom and clear feedback loops. (confidence: 0.9)
- When evaluating startups, look for founders who have a 'founder-market fit' meaning they have a deep personal connection to the problem they are solving. (confidence: 0.9)
- To build a great team, hire people who are 'scrappy' and have a bias for action, rather than those who are overly analytical. (confidence: 0.85)
- To motivate people, give them autonomy, mastery, and purpose, as seen in game design. (confidence: 0.85)
- Great people are driven by a deep curiosity and a desire to understand how things work, not just by external rewards. (confidence: 0.8)
