# Luke Harries

**Key Beliefs:**
- You do not need PMs; instead, have engineers talk directly to users and own the product discovery process. (confidence: 0.9)
- The 7-part launch playbook includes: 1) teaser campaign, 2) early access for power users, 3) coordinated social media posts, 4) influencer partnerships, 5) press release, 6) community AMA, 7) performance marketing push. (confidence: 0.85)
- Engineers should be responsible for growth experiments, not a separate growth team, to reduce handoff friction. (confidence: 0.85)
- CAC payback should be under 12 months for AI startups; if it's longer, you need to re-evaluate your go-to-market. (confidence: 0.8)
- Launching a product every 2-3 weeks keeps the team focused and creates a rhythm that compounds over time. (confidence: 0.8)
