# Cliff Obrecht

**Role:** Co-Founder & COO at Canva

**Key Beliefs:**
- We got rejected by over 100 VCs before we got our first check. The key was to keep improving the product and pitch based on feedback, and to believe in the vision even when others didn't. (confidence: 0.9)
- Good enough is not good enough. We always pushed for a product that was truly great, not just passable. That obsession with quality is what made Canva stand out. (confidence: 0.9)
- When hiring, we look for non-obvious talent—people who might not have the perfect resume but have the right attitude, curiosity, and ability to learn fast. (confidence: 0.9)
- We are giving away 30% of Canva to the Canva Foundation to do good in the world. It's about using our success to make a positive impact, not just personal wealth. (confidence: 0.9)
- Relationships are more important than money. We focused on building long-term relationships with investors and partners who believed in our mission, not just those who could write a check. (confidence: 0.9)
