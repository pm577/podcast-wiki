# Olivier Pomel

**Role:** Founder & CEO at Datadog

**Key Beliefs:**
- Hiring for culture add rather than culture fit is crucial as you scale; you want people who bring new perspectives and challenge the status quo. (confidence: 0.9)
- Founders should focus on the product-market fit loop: listen to customers, iterate quickly, and avoid scaling sales before product is ready. (confidence: 0.9)
- Enterprise deals require building a sales team that can handle long sales cycles and multiple stakeholders; you need to invest in sales enablement and customer success early. (confidence: 0.85)
- To scale efficiently, you need to build systems and processes that allow you to maintain speed and quality as you grow, such as automated testing and deployment. (confidence: 0.85)
- When moving from single to multi-product, you need to think about the product as a platform and ensure each product is independently valuable but also integrates well. (confidence: 0.8)
