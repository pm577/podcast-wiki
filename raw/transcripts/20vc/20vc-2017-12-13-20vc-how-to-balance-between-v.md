# 20VC: How To Balance Between Vision and Stubbornness, Why Speed Is The Most Important Startup Superpower & The Main Role of A Startup Founder Today with James Currier, Managing Director @ NFX

**Guest:** James Currier
**Date:** 2017-12-13
**Topics:** venture-capital, founder-psychology, go-to-market, product-strategy, marketplaces

## Summary

 James Currier is the Managing Partner @ NFX and one of Silicon Valley's foremost experts in growth and network effects. Just last week, NFX announded their latest fund, a new $150m fund to turn NFX into a significant institution.  As for James, before becoming an investor, he co-founded one of the internet's first successful user-generated companies, Tickle, in 1999. Under James' leadership, Tickle grew to become the 18th largest website in the world and was acquired by Monster for $110 million
