**Date:** 2017-02-06
**Topics:** venture-capital, founder-psychology, go-to-market, product-strategy, ai-ml

Jules Maltz, is a General Partner @ Insight Venture Partners (IVP) where he has made investments in the likes of Slack, Zendesk, Twitter, Zenefits and many more incredible companies.
