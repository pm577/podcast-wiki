# 20 VC FF 025: Crowdfunding Gimlet Media and Why The Nature Of Startup Equity Is Changing with Erin Glenn, CEO @ Betaworks' backed, Quire

**Guest:** Erin Glenn
**Date:** 2015-12-04
**Topics:** venture-capital, founder-psychology, hiring-culture, ai-ml, consumer

## Summary

Erin Glenn is the CEO @ Quire where she aims to create a way for communities to invest and collaborate with companies and their founders. Quire is changing the way companies are owned and built.
