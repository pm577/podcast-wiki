**Guest:** Crossover
**Date:** 2021-07-08
**Topics:** venture-capital, founder-psychology, go-to-market, product-strategy

## Summary

 Laela Sturdy is a General Partner @ CapitalG, Alphabet's independent growth fund with investments in the likes of Stripe, UiPath, Looker, Robinhood and Lyft to name a few. As for Laela, she joined CapitalG shortly after inception in 2013 and has led investments in Stripe, Duolingo, Gusto, UiPath and Unqork, to name a few.
