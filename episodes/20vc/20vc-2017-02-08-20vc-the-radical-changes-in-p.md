**Guest:** Randy Glein
**Date:** 2017-02-08
**Topics:** venture-capital, founder-psychology, go-to-market

## Summary

Randy Glein is a Co-Founder & Partner @ DFJ Growth where he looks to partner with entrepreneurs that have achieved market validation and are looking to scale rapidly. Randy's investments include the likes of Twitter, Tumblr, SpaceX and Tesla just to name a few.
