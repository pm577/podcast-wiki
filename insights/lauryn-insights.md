### Insight: An activation rate that falls in a lower percentage range, maybe for most compan...
**Claim:** An activation rate that falls in a lower percentage range, maybe for most companies five to 15%, is better than one that falls in a higher percentage range because it means that there's likely much higher correlation with long-term retention and you're really working hard to get most of your users to reach a state that they're not reaching today.
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** lauryn-isford
**Context:** Discussion on activation rates and their correlation with long-term retention.
