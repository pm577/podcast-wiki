### Insight: Instead of optimizing for conversion rate at each funnel stage, focus on getting...
**Claim:** Instead of optimizing for conversion rate at each funnel stage, focus on getting more people into the top of the funnel, even if it hurts conversion rates, because that can yield more total users.
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** archie-abrams
**Context:** Discussion on how teams optimize for their part of the funnel and how that can be counterproductive.
