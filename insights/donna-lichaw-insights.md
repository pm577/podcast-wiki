## Insight 9: Leadership should start with you first at the center, then extend to leading you...

**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** donna-lichaw
**Context:** Discussion about why leadership must start with the self rather than being user-centered.
