### Insight: When you're in early customer conversations, pay attention to the type of questi...
**Claim:** When you're in early customer conversations, pay attention to the type of questions you get. If customers ask 'How are you pricing this?' or 'When can we start a POV?', that's a strong signal of product-market fit. If they just say 'interesting' and don't ask actionable next steps, it's a warning sign.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** raaz-herzberg
**Context:** Raaz describes how the shift in customer questions from vague interest to specific buying questions signaled product-market fit.
