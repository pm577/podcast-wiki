## Insight 1: So much of the way that we tackle problems and build products is this builder mi...
**Claim:** So much of the way that we tackle problems and build products is this builder mindset. It's like I have a plan. I then manipulate things to match my plan and make it happen. And this is a way you can create tons of value. Part of the problem though is it can't possibly create more value than the effort that you put into it. What I look for instead are things that can be gardened, things that can grow on their own and that you can direct or maybe give a little bit of extra energy to or curate over, and is a totally different mindset for it.
**Framework:** Gardener vs. Builder
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** alex-komoroske
**Context:** Opening discussion on product mindset
