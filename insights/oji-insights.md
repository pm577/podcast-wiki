### Insight: Build a great product that solves a sharp problem. Synthetic virality fails if t...
**Claim:** Build a great product that solves a sharp problem. Synthetic virality fails if the product sucks; true virality comes from users loving the product and spreading it organically.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** oji-udezue
**Context:** Oji discusses how Slack achieved virality without synthetic tricks, by building a product that people loved and talked about.

### Insight: To find big B2B SaaS ideas, evaluate the workflow along two dimensions: how many...
**Claim:** To find big B2B SaaS ideas, evaluate the workflow along two dimensions: how many departments it applies to (everyone vs. niche) and how frequently it's executed (daily vs. infrequent). The most promising quadrants are high-frequency everyone (e.g., Slack, email) and high-frequency niche (e.g., Jira, Salesforce).
**Framework:** Workflow Quadrant Framework
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** oji-udezue
**Context:** Oji explains his framework for predicting startup success based on workflow breadth and frequency.

**Framework:** Workflow Quadrant Framework
