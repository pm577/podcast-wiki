## Insight 1: To measure product-market fit scientifically, use the Sean Ellis survey question...
**Claim:** To measure product-market fit scientifically, use the Sean Ellis survey question: 'How would you feel if you could no longer use the product?' with responses on a scale. The percentage who say 'very disappointed' is the PMF score.
**Framework:** Sean Ellis PMF Survey
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2020-06-05-20vc-how-to-scientifically-me
**Context:** Discussion on scientifically measuring product-market fit
