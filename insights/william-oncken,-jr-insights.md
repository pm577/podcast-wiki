# William Oncken, Jr. — Insights

*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: When a subordinate brings a problem to a manager and the manager says 'Let me th...
**Claim:** When a subordinate brings a problem to a manager and the manager says 'Let me think about it and I'll let you know,' the monkey (responsibility) leaps from the subordinate's back to the manager's. The manager has voluntarily assumed a subordinate position.
**Framework:** Who's Got the Monkey
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** essential-reading-for-product-builders-part-1
**Context:** Discussion of the essay 'Who's Got the Monkey' about delegation and responsibility management.

## Insight 2: Agency is an amalgamation of autonomy (capacity to formulate autonomous goals) a...
**Claim:** Agency is an amalgamation of autonomy (capacity to formulate autonomous goals) and efficacy (ability and willingness to pursue those goals). The opposite is doing what you're supposed to do or being passive in the face of problems.
**Framework:** On Agency
**Audience:** general | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** essential-reading-for-product-builders-part-1
**Context:** Discussion of the essay 'On agency' by Henrik Karlsson.

**Source:** essential-reading-for-product-builders-part-1
