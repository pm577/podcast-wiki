## Insight 2: Network effects are not just about getting more users; they are about increasing...
**Claim:** Network effects are not just about getting more users; they are about increasing the value of the network for each user as more users join. The right way to think about network effects is to focus on the density of interactions within the network, not just the number of users.
**Framework:** Network Effects
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.85
**Source:** 20vc-2018-03-05-20vc-why-how-and-when-to-thi
**Context:** Discussion on network effects in marketplaces
