### Insight: Fundraising has two levers: FOMO (fear of missing out) and leverage. To create F...
**Claim:** Fundraising has two levers: FOMO (fear of missing out) and leverage. To create FOMO, you need to create a competitive dynamic among investors, e.g., by having multiple term sheets or creating a deadline. Leverage comes from having a strong business that investors want to be part of.
**Framework:** FOMO & Leverage
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2018-03-09-20vc-2-levers-to-fundraising
**Context:** Discussion on fundraising strategies and how to use psychological levers to improve outcomes.

### Insight: To implement a sustainable growth mindset, focus on unit economics and customer ...
**Claim:** To implement a sustainable growth mindset, focus on unit economics and customer acquisition cost (CAC) relative to lifetime value (LTV). Don't just grow at all costs; ensure that growth is profitable or has a clear path to profitability.
**Framework:** Sustainable Growth Mindset
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.85
**Source:** 20vc-2018-03-09-20vc-2-levers-to-fundraising
**Context:** Advice on balancing growth with financial health, avoiding the trap of burning cash without a plan.

### Insight: Put VCs to work for you by being specific about what you need from them. Ask for...
**Claim:** Put VCs to work for you by being specific about what you need from them. Ask for introductions to potential customers, partners, or hires, and leverage their network and expertise. Don't just take their money; actively manage the relationship.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2018-03-09-20vc-2-levers-to-fundraising
**Context:** Discussion on how founders can maximize the value of their investors beyond capital.

### Insight: When fundraising, create a sense of urgency by setting a timeline. For example, ...
**Claim:** When fundraising, create a sense of urgency by setting a timeline. For example, tell investors you are closing the round in two weeks. This forces them to make decisions quickly and can create FOMO.
**Framework:** FOMO & Leverage
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 10/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** 20vc-2018-03-09-20vc-2-levers-to-fundraising
**Context:** Practical tactic for accelerating the fundraising process and increasing investor interest.
