# Pete Flint — Insights

*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: The three leading indicators that suggest potential marketplace success are: 1) ...
**Claim:** The three leading indicators that suggest potential marketplace success are: 1) the marketplace must provide a fundamentally better user experience than the offline alternative, 2) the marketplace must have a high degree of liquidity (supply and demand must be balanced), and 3) the marketplace must have a strong network effects moat.
**Framework:** Three Leading Indicators for Marketplace Success
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2018-07-09-20vc-lessons-from-2x-bn-exit
**Context:** Discussion of what makes a marketplace successful
