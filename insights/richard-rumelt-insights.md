**Claim:** Don't call it strategy, call it an action agenda. Begin to try to identify the one or two key challenges that can actually be addressed and what are we going to do about it? What are the coherent actions we're going to do to take these on?

**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** richard-rumelt
**Context:** Richard Rumelt discussing the essence of strategic thinking and the need to focus on actionable challenges.
