### Insight: Early in your career, focus on getting stuff out and done (outputs) rather than ...
**Claim:** Early in your career, focus on getting stuff out and done (outputs) rather than obsessing over impact. Show your work through artifacts like PRDs, product notes, and strategy docs.
**Framework:** Three W's (What you produce, What you bring to the table, What's your operating model)
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** vikrama-dhiman
**Context:** Vikrama explains that early-career PMs should focus on outputs (shipping products, sourcing content) rather than solely on outcomes or impact.
