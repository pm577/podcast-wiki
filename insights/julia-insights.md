### Insight: M&A is always about creating plan Bs. For any one company, there's only ever two...
**Claim:** M&A is always about creating plan Bs. For any one company, there's only ever two to three buyers that find what you're building to be extremely strategic. To get noticed, figure out the area where you bring a competitive advantage and inflict pain on that potential buyer in a way that's still friendly and open, making it impossible for them to ignore you.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** julia-schottenstein
**Context:** Discussion on M&A strategy and how to get noticed by potential acquirers.
