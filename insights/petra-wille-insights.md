**Claim:** Getting promoted is way harder if you're not good at telling stories and rallying the team behind the shared goal. You usually achieve this through good storytelling techniques. In some teams, the product person not being really good at it can be compensated by the team, but I would consider it a bit of a career staller if you don't get to a decent level of storytelling and public speaking.

**Source:** petra-wille
**Context:** Discussion on the importance of storytelling for product managers' career progression.
