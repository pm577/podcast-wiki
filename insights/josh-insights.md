### Insight: Focus on a single key metric that captures retention, engagement, and growth, su...
**Claim:** Focus on a single key metric that captures retention, engagement, and growth, such as D5/D7 (how many people use the product at least five days a week). This metric cannot be gamed and tracks true habitual usage.
**Framework:** D5/D7 metric
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** josh-miller
**Context:** Josh explains how The Browser Company tracks growth and retention by obsessing over how many people use Arc five out of seven days a week.
