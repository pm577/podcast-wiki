# Nikita Bier — Insights

*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: There are only a few core reasons why people download apps: finding a mate, maki...
**Claim:** There are only a few core reasons why people download apps: finding a mate, making or saving money, and unplugging from reality.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 5/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** how-to-consistently-go-viral-nikita-bie
**Context:** Discussion of basic human motivations for app downloads

## Insight 2: Optimize for the aha moment in seconds. Demonstrate core value within the first ...
**Claim:** Optimize for the aha moment in seconds. Demonstrate core value within the first three seconds of using an app by ruthlessly cutting features and being creative with APIs.
**Audience:** engineer | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** how-to-consistently-go-viral-nikita-bie
**Context:** Advice on shrinking attention spans and first-time user experience

## Insight 3: The number of invitations sent per user drops 20% for every additional year of a...
**Claim:** The number of invitations sent per user drops 20% for every additional year of age from 13 to 18. Focus on demographics with high urgency and frequent interactions, like teens, for network-effect products.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** how-to-consistently-go-viral-nikita-bie
**Context:** Discussion of why teens are valuable for growth
