## Insight 1: When giving feedback, avoid starting sentences with 'you' or 'why' because they ...
**Claim:** When giving feedback, avoid starting sentences with 'you' or 'why' because they trigger defensiveness. Instead, use 'what', 'when', 'where', 'how' to keep the conversation open.
**Audience:** general | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** carole-robin
**Context:** Discussion on how to give feedback well and avoid defensiveness.
