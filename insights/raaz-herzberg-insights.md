# Raaz Herzberg — Insights

*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: When you're in early customer conversations, pay attention to the type of questi...
**Claim:** When you're in early customer conversations, pay attention to the type of questions you get. If they ask 'How are you pricing this?' or 'When can we start a POV?', that's a strong signal of interest. If they just say 'interesting' and move on, it's likely not real enthusiasm.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** raaz-herzberg
**Context:** Raaz describing the shift in customer questions when they found product-market fit for Wiz's cloud security pivot.

## Insight 2: As a product manager, if you don't understand what you're building after weeks o...

**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
