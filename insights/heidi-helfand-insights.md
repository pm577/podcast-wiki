## Insight 1: Reteaming is hard. Reorgs are hard. You can't lump them all into one thing with ...
**Claim:** Reteaming is hard. Reorgs are hard. You can't lump them all into one thing with oh, it's all great all the time. No, it's not. If we could just build the software, deliver to the customer, get the product market fit, hey, have we delighted them or not? If only it could be that easy. No, we have the people layer, so let's focus there too.
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 5/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** heidi-helfand
**Context:** Opening remarks about the challenge of reteaming and the importance of the people layer.
