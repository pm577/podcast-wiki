# Rachel Lockett — Insights

*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: Great leaders know that when you try to advise and have the answer all the time,...
**Claim:** Great leaders know that when you try to advise and have the answer all the time, you're not actually equipping your team to go solve the hard problems. You're training your team to come to you with all of the hard problems.
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** lenny-2025-11-23-rachel-lockett
**Context:** Discussion of the biggest gap leaders have: knowing when to coach vs. tell
