# Judd — Insights

*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

### Insight: User-centered performance refers to work done to signal customer obsession rathe...
**Claim:** User-centered performance refers to work done to signal customer obsession rather than to learn or make different decisions. It's common, e.g., when a PM asks a researcher to validate assumptions at the end of a product process when it's too late to change anything.
**Framework:** User-centered performance
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** judd-antin
**Context:** Judd defines user-centered performance and gives an example of PMs asking for validation at the end of the process.

### Insight: Researchers should adopt a falsification mindset: 'We don't validate, we falsify...

**Framework:** Falsification mindset
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** judd-antin
**Context:** Judd explains his mantra of falsification over validation.
