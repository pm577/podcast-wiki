**Claim:** Just as Betty Crocker found success by letting users add eggs to their cake mix, your AI tools should foster a sense of ownership. Avoid full automation; instead, integrate features that allow users to actively participate in the experience.
**Framework:** IKEA effect
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.85
**Source:** aman-khan-arize-ai-ex-spotify-apple-cruise-1
**Context:** Advice on designing AI products that engage users rather than fully automating tasks.
