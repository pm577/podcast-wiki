*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: SPACs provide a faster and more certain path to going public compared to traditi...

**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.85
**Source:** 20vc-2020-08-31-20vc-spacs-what-are-they-wh

## Insight 3: SPACs allow investors to participate in the upside of a merger without the risk ...

**Audience:** investor | **Actionability:** 6/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2020-08-31-20vc-spacs-what-are-they-wh
**Context:** Explanation of SPAC mechanics for investors
