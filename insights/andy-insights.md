**Claim:** The traditional pitch structure (problem-pain-solution) is like an 'arrogant doctor' and sets you up for bragging; instead, start with a shift in the world from an old game to a new game.
**Framework:** Strategic narrative
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** andy-raskin
**Context:** Andy contrasts the typical business school pitch with the movie-inspired structure that starts with a world shift.
