*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

### Insight: Founders should not be the one to create the sales playbook because they are too...
**Claim:** Founders should not be the one to create the sales playbook because they are too close to the product and have a bias that prevents them from seeing the sales process objectively.
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2022-06-22-20-sales-why-founders-should
**Context:** Discussion on why founders should not create the sales playbook

### Insight: Structure each interview in the hiring process for sales reps to assess specific...
**Claim:** Structure each interview in the hiring process for sales reps to assess specific competencies: first interview for sales skills, second for culture fit, third for problem-solving, and use a panel to get multiple perspectives.
**Framework:** Interview panel
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2022-06-22-20-sales-why-founders-should
**Context:** How to structure each interview in the hiring process for sales reps

### Insight: Use an interview panel effectively by having each interviewer focus on a differe...
**Claim:** Use an interview panel effectively by having each interviewer focus on a different dimension (e.g., sales acumen, cultural alignment, technical knowledge) and then debrief together to avoid groupthink.
**Framework:** Interview panel
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2022-06-22-20-sales-why-founders-should
**Context:** How to use an interview panel effectively
