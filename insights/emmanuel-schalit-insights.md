**Framework:** Board management
**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2019-12-06-20vc-why-raising-a-mega-round

**Audience:** founder | **Actionability:** 6/10 | **Confidence:** 0.75
