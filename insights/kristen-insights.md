### Insight: People make decisions with lots of emotion, are present-biased, over-weigh their...
**Claim:** People make decisions with lots of emotion, are present-biased, over-weigh their present selves, and follow social norms, but they do these things in predictable ways. Once you understand how and why people behave, you can start to change it.
**Framework:** Behavioral economics
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** kristen-berman
**Context:** Kristen explaining what behavioral economics is and how it can be applied to change behavior.

### Insight: Budgeting features often fail to change spending behavior because they require t...
**Claim:** Budgeting features often fail to change spending behavior because they require too many steps: knowing your budget, knowing where you are in the month, making a plan, and executing multiple times. A behavioral diagnosis mapping out each step reveals why it's unlikely to work.
**Framework:** Behavioral diagnosis
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 9/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** kristen-berman
**Context:** Discussion of a FinTech app where the most requested feature (budgeting) had zero impact on spend.

**Framework:** Defaults and rules of thumb

**Context:** Kristen suggesting better approaches to personal finance behavior change after the budgeting failure.
