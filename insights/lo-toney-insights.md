*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

## Insight 1: Fundraising for funds has changed in COVID: LPs are more focused on track record...
**Claim:** Fundraising for funds has changed in COVID: LPs are more focused on track record and differentiation, and are doing more diligence on fund managers' ability to navigate downturns.
**Audience:** investor | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.8
**Source:** 20vc-2020-06-26-20vc-how-fundraising-for-fund
**Context:** Discussion on how fundraising for venture funds has evolved during the pandemic.

**Framework:** Minimum Viable Fund Size
**Audience:** investor | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** 20vc-2020-06-26-20vc-how-fundraising-for-fund
**Context:** Advice on how to determine the smallest fund size that makes sense for a new fund manager.
