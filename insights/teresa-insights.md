*Extracted from podcast appearances.*

### Insight: Most product teams don't know how to manage the complex problem of starting from...
**Claim:** Most product teams don't know how to manage the complex problem of starting from an outcome and figuring out what to build; they need scaffolding like the opportunity solution tree to add structure to this wide open, messy problem.
**Framework:** Opportunity Solution Tree
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 7/10 | **Confidence:** 0.95
**Source:** teresa-torres
**Context:** Teresa explains the purpose of the opportunity solution tree framework, noting that teams are used to being told what to build and need help shifting to outcome-focused work.

**Framework:** Opportunity Solution Tree
**Audience:** operator | **Actionability:** 8/10 | **Confidence:** 0.9
**Source:** teresa-torres
**Context:** Teresa discusses the common mistake of writing opportunities as solutions and emphasizes the importance of staying in the problem space.
