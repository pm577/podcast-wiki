# Dylan Field

*Co-founder and CEO of Figma. Founded in 2012, Figma grew from a design tool into a comprehensive platform used by every modern product team. Known for pioneering browser-based collaborative design.*

## Key Views

1. **Design is art applied to problem solving**: Pure art without user need lacks purpose. Pure problem-solving without creativity lacks soul. The magic is combining creative expression with solving a real user need. This balance is what makes design a differentiator rather than a commodity.

2. **AI makes craft and quality the new moat**: As AI generates more software automatically, the differentiating factor becomes unique design and high craft. When anyone can create the "obvious" solution with AI, the winners will be those who invest in quality, taste, and distinctive experience. The bar for design rises, not falls.

3. **Intuition is a hypothesis generator, not truth**: Dylan treats his intuition as generating hypotheses that need testing, not as inherently correct. This prevents the arrogance of "founder's gut" while still leveraging the pattern-matching that experience provides. He constantly refines taste through exposure to great design.

4. **Simplifying is an ongoing operational challenge**: Keeping Figma simple as it grows into a platform requires constant vigilance. Simplification isn't a one-time project — it's a muscle that must be exercised continuously. Every new feature risks complexity, so the team must actively prune and consolidate.

5. **Community connection is more energizing than product success**: Asked about Config highlights, Dylan immediately cites the community — not the product launches or demos. The deep connection with users who love the tool is what sustains him through the exhaustion of building and leading. Community is the ultimate moat for a creative tool.
