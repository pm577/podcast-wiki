# Anton Osika

Anton Osika appeared on Lenny's Podcast to discuss building lovable: $10m arr in 60 days with 15 people.

## Key Views

**The last piece of software — instant from idea to working product** — Anton's vision for Lovable: a tool so powerful that going from idea to a fully working, integrated product is nearly instantaneous. "It's almost instant to go from what you want to change in the product or what product you want to build to having it fully working end-to-end, integrated with any of your existing systems." ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/anton-osika.md]

$10M ARR in 60 days with 15 people — extreme capital efficiency** — Lovable achieved one of the fastest growth trajectories in European startup history by staying tiny and focused. The key was not premature scaling but obsessing over reliability. "Not getting stuck is the most important thing for people. And that's why we entered this space late, we haven't done the same type of marketing as many others." ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/anton-osika.md]

**Generalist > specialist in the AI era — every hire must wear multiple hats** — Anton hires for raw cognitive capability and generalist thinking. His ideal candidate has product taste, engineering understanding, user empathy, and the ability to quickly learn any new skill. "Being a generalist is much more important than it used to be." ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/anton-osika.md]

**Weekly planning with no fixed roadmap — identify the biggest bottleneck, solve it, move on** — Lovable's planning cadence: identify the single biggest bottleneck, solve it aggressively that week, then find the next. No multi-quarter roadmaps, no heavy process. "Not overthinking, not dreaming out the long roadmap — there's a very simple algorithm." ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/anton-osika.md]

**The Cambrian explosion of entrepreneurship — everyone can now build** — When building software becomes as easy as describing what you want, the number of people who can start a tech company explodes. "Everyone who has an idea is going to say, 'Okay, I'm going to build this thing and show you that this is the best version of the product.'" ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/anton-osika.md]
