# Lazar Jovanovic

Lazar Jovanovic appeared on Lenny's Podcast to discuss the rise of the professional vibe coder (a new ai-era job).

## Key Views

**First Official Vibe Coder** — 'I'm the first official vibe coding engineer at Lovable.' His job: build both internal tools and customer-facing products purely using AI, without a coding background. 'It became a job by building in public. You don't need a company to hire you — you can hire yourself as a professional vibe coder first.' ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-08-lazar-jovanovic.md]

**Not Knowing Code is an Advantage** — 'People like me don't know that they're not supposed to be building X, Y, Z. And that's how we actually are able to build it.' When told Chrome extensions couldn't be built with Lovable (different stack), he just prompted and built one. Technical constraints only exist if you know them. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-08-lazar-jovanovic.md]

**Coding Will Become Calligraphy** — 'Coding is going to be like calligraphy. People will say 'Oh my God, you wrote that code? That's so amazing.' It's going to be so rare that it becomes an art.' The mechanical parts become obsolete; the craft becomes a niche. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-08-lazar-jovanovic.md]

**AI is an Amplifier, Not a Replacement** — 'AI, regardless of your background, is an amplifier. If you don't know what you're doing, you're just going to produce garbage faster.' Vibe coding works because he has good judgment, clarity, and taste — not because AI replaces these. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-08-lazar-jovanovic.md]

**The Aladdin Genie Framework** — 'AI just doesn't understand what you mean when you say 'You know what I mean?' You need to be specific.' Like Aladdin asking to be taller and the genie making him 13 feet tall. Clarity of ask is the core skill. 'I'm optimizing 100% of my time today on good judgment, clarity, quality, taste.' ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-08-lazar-jovanovic.md]
