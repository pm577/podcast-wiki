# Alex Bouaziz

Co-Founder & CEO @ Deel. Scaled Deel from $57M to $297M ARR in 12 months, reaching $1BN+ ARR with EBITDA positive since Sept 2022, 85%+ gross margins. Raised over $679M at a $12.1BN valuation from a16z, Spark Capital, Coatue.

## Key Views

### 1. Speed of Execution is the Most Important Factor to Success

The single biggest advantage in scaling Deel was speed — making decisions fast, executing relentlessly, and hiring ahead of the curve rather than waiting for proof points before scaling.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2023-02-01-20vc-from-57m-in-arr-to-297.md]

### 2. Hiring 2,000 People Remotely in 3 Years is a Competitive Moats

Deel's ability to hire and onboard talent globally, fully remotely, at massive scale was a core operational capability that most companies cannot replicate. This allowed Deel to grow headcount faster than competitors while maintaining culture.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2023-02-01-20vc-from-57m-in-arr-to-297.md]

### 3. Secondaries: Why, When and How Much to Take Out

Bouaziz advocates for founders taking secondary liquidity at the right moments — not too early to lose incentive, but not too late that they become risk-averse. The amount and timing should align with long-term company building, not personal enrichment.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2023-02-01-20vc-from-57m-in-arr-to-297.md]

### 4. Raising $300M+ at a $17BN Valuation is About Momentum

In his later appearance, Bouaziz discussed how the $300M raise at $17BN was driven by performance — Deel had just had its first $100M revenue month and had been profitable for over 3 years, making the raise a strategic move rather than a necessity.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-10-22-20vc-deel-ceo-alex-bouaziz-on.md]
