# Kyle Norton

Chief Revenue Officer @ Owner.com. Scaled revenue from $2M to $40M ARR in under 3 years while selling to one of the toughest markets: SMB restaurants. Previously led sales at Shopify, where he helped architect one of the most operationally elite GTM orgs in SaaS.

## Key Views

### 1. From $2M ARR to $40M ARR: The Playbook

Scaling revenue 20x in 3 years in SMB requires a fundamentally different approach than enterprise. Speed of response, volume of outreach, and simplicity of messaging matter more than complex sales cycles. The playbook involves aggressive outbound paired with a refined inbound motion.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-05-30-20sales-from-2m-arr-to-40m.md]

### 2. Why Founders Who Skip Sales Get Burned

Founders who delegate sales too early without understanding the customer conversation lose the ability to refine product positioning, pricing, and ICP. The best founders personally close the first 10-20 customers to build intuition before scaling a sales team.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-05-30-20sales-from-2m-arr-to-40m.md]

### 3. 90% Inbound, Then 70% Outbound — Neither Alone is Enough

The mix shifts over time. Early on, inbound dominates (90%) as product-market fit is validated. Later, outbound must take over (up to 70%) to hit aggressive growth targets. Companies that fail to transition from inbound to outbound stall.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-05-30-20sales-from-2m-arr-to-40m.md]

### 4. Why Pipeline Reviews are BS

Traditional pipeline reviews are backward-looking and waste time. Instead, focus on forward-looking deal inspection: what needs to happen for a deal to close, what's the actual buying signal, and what's the real next step? Reviews should be coaching moments, not status updates.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-05-30-20sales-from-2m-arr-to-40m.md]

### 5. The Sales Call Script that Closes 99% of Prospects

A repeatable script based on deep customer understanding — listen more than talk, diagnose before prescribing, and handle objections with empathy. The script is not about manipulation; it's about guiding the prospect to their own conclusion that your solution is the right one.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-05-30-20sales-from-2m-arr-to-40m.md]
