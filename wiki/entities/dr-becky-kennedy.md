# Dr. Becky Kennedy

Dr. Becky Kennedy appeared on Lenny's Podcast to discuss a child psychologist's guide to working with difficult adults.

## Key Views

**Repair is the #1 Relationship Strategy** — Secure attachment isn't defined by getting it right all the time — it's defined by an adult who's willing to repair after messing up. 'The thing that really differentiated secure attachment... was the presence of repair.' Apologizing to coworkers after a harsh meeting rebuilds trust faster than never making mistakes. 'Perfect is creepy.' ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-01-dr-becky-kennedy.md]

**Boundaries vs Requests** — 'Boundaries are what you tell someone else you will do, and it requires the other person to do nothing. Making a request, that's not a boundary.' Most people confuse requests with boundaries. A boundary is 'If you speak to me that way, I will leave the room' — not 'Please stop speaking to me that way.' ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-01-dr-becky-kennedy.md]

**All Humans Need the Same Things** — Bad behavior is a skill deficit, not a character flaw. 'When you look at bad behavior, the actual problem is someone doesn't have the skill they need to manage something happening internally.' Parenting and leadership principles are interchangeable — both are about understanding how humans operate in systems. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-01-dr-becky-kennedy.md]

**Resilience Over Happiness** — 'Our whole parenting philosophy is resilience over happiness.' For resilient work cultures, build people who can say 'This is hard and I can do hard things.' Short-term happiness at the expense of long-term capability is a leadership trap. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-01-dr-becky-kennedy.md]

**The Most Generous Interpretation** — Separate behavior from identity. 'Someone's late to work a lot — 'Oh, that person's lazy.' The quickest way to have an unproductive conversation is to lose sight of the fact that someone's good inside.' Lead with curiosity over judgment. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-02-01-dr-becky-kennedy.md]
