# Matt Clifford

Co-Founder of Entrepreneur First (EF), the leading global talent investor and incubator. EF has incubated startups worth over $10BN, including Cleo, Tractable, and Aztec Protocol. Appointed Chair of ARIA (the UK's Advanced Research and Invention Agency). Advises the UK government on AI and served as the Prime Minister's Representative for the AI Safety Summit at Bletchley Park in 2023.

## Key Views

### 1. Why More People Should Be Entrepreneurs

Clifford's founding thesis with Entrepreneur First is that more people should be entrepreneurs, particularly top technical talent. He discusses how to know when you are ready to start a company, arguing that the traditional "wait until you have the perfect idea" approach is flawed — instead, the right approach is to find co-founders first and let the idea emerge from the team dynamic.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2016-05-11-20vc-why-more-people-should-b.md]

### 2. LLMs Are Reaching Diminishing Returns

Clifford argues that large language models are approaching a stage of diminishing returns, and the next S-curve in AI will come from a different paradigm. He discusses the bull and bear case for China's ability to challenge US AI capabilities, how AI changes the future of warfare, and how AI agents will fundamentally reshape society in ways that go beyond just better chatbots.
