# Amit Bendov

Co-Founder & CEO @ Gong, the leading AI-sales platform. Gong has raised over $600M from Sequoia, Thrive, Salesforce and more. Surpassed $300M in ARR, serves thousands of customers (including multiple Fortune 10s), and is valued at over $7BN.

## Key Views

### 1. Why AI SDRs are BS and Do Not Work

Amit argues that most AI SDR (Sales Development Representative) tools fail because they automate the wrong part of the process — spammy outreach that doesn't understand context or buying signals. AI should be used to analyze and coach sales conversations, not to generate more low-quality prospecting volume.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-09-12-20vc-why-ai-sdrs-are-bs-and-d.md]

### 2. How to Use AI in Your Sales Team and Process to Win

The winning approach is using AI to capture, transcribe, and analyze every customer conversation — then feeding that intelligence back into coaching, forecasting, and deal strategy. AI makes every rep better by showing them what actually works based on real data, not gut feel.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-09-12-20vc-why-ai-sdrs-are-bs-and-d.md]

### 3. What Skills All New Reps Need in an AI-First World

Reps need to be better communicators, better listeners, and more analytical. The commoditized parts of sales (cold calling scripts, basic discovery) get automated. What remains is strategic thinking, relationship building, and the ability to act on AI-generated insights.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-09-12-20vc-why-ai-sdrs-are-bs-and-d.md]

### 4. Why CRM Was Always a Lie

Traditional CRMs rely on reps manually entering data, which is inherently unreliable. Gong's insight was that the truth of a deal lives in the conversation, not the CRM field. Companies that depend on CRM data alone are making decisions on fiction.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2025-09-12-20vc-why-ai-sdrs-are-bs-and-d.md]
