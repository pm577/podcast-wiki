# Claire Vo

*Chief Product Officer at LaunchDarkly, founder of ChatPRD. Former CPO at Color and Optimizely. Pioneered the CPTO (Chief Product & Technology Officer) model combining product and engineering.*

## Key Views

1. **Know what you want and make it easy for your boss to say yes**: Claire's career progression from copywriter to CPO came from always knowing her next role and being explicit about it. When the head of marketing left, she walked into her boss's office with an org chart already drawn and said "here's how I solve this problem." She frames promotions around what's good for the company, not what's good for her.

2. **Bring the clock speed one click faster**: At later-stage companies, Claire is hired not to make them operate like big companies but to remind them they can operate like startups. Her rule: if you think something needs to be done this year, it needs to be done this half. She pushes teams to move one click faster than feels comfortable.

3. **AI won't replace PMs, but it will shift the skill set**: Claire built ChatPRD as a nights-and-weekends project, and it became the most popular PM-specific AI tool. She believes AI will complement PMs rather than eliminate them in the near term, but the skills required will shift much faster than many anticipate.

4. **The CPTO model eliminates "what's best for product vs. engineering" debates**: Running product, engineering, and design together under one leader removes artificial trade-offs. The only question becomes "what is best for the organization?" This model creates faster decision-making and shared ownership of outcomes.

5. **Career conversations should be rare and focused on company needs**: Only ~0.005% of Claire's interactions with her boss are about her career growth. Instead of constantly pushing for promotion, she focuses on doing the work and producing results. The conversation is about "what a different position does for the company," not "what it does for me."
