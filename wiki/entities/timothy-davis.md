# Timothy Davis

Timothy Davis appeared on Lenny's Podcast to discuss the ultimate guide to paid growth — timothy davis (shopify) on paid marketing strategy.

## Key Views

**Paid Is for Everyone** — Timothy argues paid marketing is no longer optional because platforms have shifted toward pay-for-play. Google's AI overviews and paid ads take the first four spots, and organic visibility on Meta is nearly nonexistent without promoted posts. He recommends everyone at minimum do paid search since it is user-driven (users type in keywords), unlike disruptive media. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/timothy-davis.md]

**Signs of Life Testing** — Instead of going all-in on a new platform, run small 'signs of life' tests. Load existing customer data to build lookalike audiences (starting with 1% — the closest match), put a small budget behind it, and see if there is a positive signal before building full campaigns. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/timothy-davis.md]

**Signal vs. Noise in Data** — Drawing from Nate Silver's 'The Signal and the Noise,' Timothy emphasizes hiring people who can distinguish signal from noise in the overwhelming amount of platform data. He can teach anyone to run Google/Meta ads, but the hard part is knowing which metrics matter based on campaign goals. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/timothy-davis.md]

**Creative Is Massively Underestimated** — Creative quality has enormous impact on ad performance. Timothy cites Dollar Shave Club's viral video as an example of creative that transcends targeting. He advises starting with emotion (comedy, happiness) to create lasting connections, and notes that in-house creative outperforms agencies because it allows faster iteration. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/timothy-davis.md]

**Forward Thinking, Backwards Planning** — Start by defining where you want to be (e.g., on all platforms), then work backwards to figure out the milestones: start with search (keywords only, no creative needed), then add video, then expand to Meta, TikTok, etc. This approach helps teams methodically scale paid growth with clear goals at each stage. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/timothy-davis.md]
