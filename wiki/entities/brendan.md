# Brendan Foody

Brendan Foody is the CEO and co-founder of Mercor, the fastest-growing company in history to go from $1M to $500M in revenue (in just 17 months). At 22, he became the youngest American unicorn founder. Mercor is an AI evaluation marketplace serving the world's top AI labs.

## Key Views

### The Era of Evals — The Biggest Business Opportunity in History
Brendan argues we're entering the "era of evals." The wealthiest companies in the world (AI labs) will spend whatever it takes to improve model capabilities. What they need is a labor marketplace of extraordinary professionals who can measure model capabilities — experts who can evaluate whether a model's outputs are correct across thousands of domains. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/brendan-foody.md]

### $1M to $500M in 17 Months
Mercor achieved the fastest growth trajectory in history by serving all top AI labs simultaneously. The business model: connecting domain experts (lawyers, doctors, mathematicians, coders) to AI labs that need their expertise for model evaluation and reinforcement learning. Each evaluation task is a mini-consulting engagement with an AI lab. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/brendan-foody.md]

### Experts as the Key Constraint in AI Progress
The limiting factor in AI development is no longer compute or data — it's access to high-quality human expertise for evaluation. AI labs need PhD-level physicists to evaluate physics reasoning, attorneys to evaluate legal reasoning, and medical professionals to evaluate diagnostic accuracy. Mercor's marketplace solves this bottleneck. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/brendan-foody.md]

### The Youngest American Unicorn Founder
Brendan's age (22 when reaching unicorn status) provides a unique perspective. His approach combines the ambition of youth with a mature understanding of market dynamics. He emphasizes that the AI evaluation market is not a temporary opportunity — as models improve, the need for high-quality evaluation only increases. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/brendan-foody.md]

### Evals as Infrastructure for AI Safety
Beyond just business opportunity, the evaluation ecosystem is critical for AI safety. Labs need rigorous, independent evaluation of model capabilities and risks. Mercor's marketplace provides the infrastructure for this, creating a competitive advantage through the breadth and depth of expert talent they can mobilize. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/brendan-foody.md]
