# Mike Seibel

Co-Founder and CEO of Justin.TV (later Twitch.TV, acquired by Amazon for $970M) and SocialCam (acquired by Autodesk for $60M). Y Combinator Winter 2007 alumnus. Founding partner and Managing Director of Y Combinator's startup program.

## Key Views

### 1. The Twitch Journey: From Lifecasting to $970M Amazon Acquisition

Justin.TV started as a lifecasting experiment — a camera strapped to Justin Kan's hat broadcasting 24/7. Mike and his team pivoted repeatedly until they found product-market fit in live gaming broadcasts, becoming Twitch. The $970M acquisition by Amazon validated that persistence through multiple pivots can lead to extraordinary outcomes. YC W07 was one of YC's earliest batches, and Justin.TV was a standout.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2015-03-02-20-vc-016-y-combinator-twitc.md]

### 2. SocialCam: Building and Exiting in Mobile Video

Mike also co-founded SocialCam, a mobile video sharing app that grew rapidly and was acquired by Autodesk for $60M in 2012. The experience taught him the dynamics of consumer mobile growth: speed of execution, riding platform waves (in this case, the early iPhone), and knowing when to sell.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2015-03-02-20-vc-016-y-combinator-twitc.md]
