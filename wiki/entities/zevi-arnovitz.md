# Zevi Arnovitz

Product Manager at Meta, previously at Wix. Known for being a non-technical PM who has mastered AI-assisted development using Cursor, Claude Code, and ChatGPT Projects to build real, production-grade products. His workflow has become a blueprint for non-technical people who want to build software with AI.

## Key Views

### 1. AI Gives Non-Technical People Superpowers

Zevi has "zero technical background" — studied music, no army tech unit in Israel. When Sonnet 3.5 came out, he watched a video of someone building apps with Bolt/Lovable and felt "like someone came up to me and said, 'You have superpowers now.'" He didn't even unpack his bags from a trip before starting to build. The core insight: titles are going to collapse as everyone becomes a builder.^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-01-18-zevi-arnovitz.md]

### 2. Create a "CTO" Persona to Counter AI's Sycophancy

AI coding tools are too eager to please — they start coding immediately, which creates gnarly bugs. Zevi created a separate ChatGPT project with a "CTO" persona: "I own the problem. You're the complete technical owner. Challenge me. Don't be a people pleaser." This agent plans the architecture before writing code, preventing the chaos of AI rushing to implement. Planning is critical before any technical work.^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-01-18-zevi-arnovitz.md]

### 3. AI Reviewing AI: Use Multiple Models for Code Review

Since Zevi can't review code himself, he has multiple AIs review each other's work. His workflow: use Cursor's `/review` command to have Claude review its own code, then use a different model (Codex) to review the same code independently. Different models catch different mistakes. This multi-model review pattern compensates for his lack of technical expertise.^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-01-18-zevi-arnovitz.md]

### 4. The Best Time to Be a Junior Is Now (Contrarian)

Contrary to the narrative that AI is eliminating junior roles, Zevi argues the opposite: "When else in history could you get out of school and just build a startup on your own?" AI compresses the time from idea to working product so dramatically that juniors can now do what previously required a full engineering team. Engineers at Meta ask him to teach them his AI workflow.^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-01-18-zevi-arnovitz.md]

### 5. Compartmentalize AI with Projects (Not Memory)

ChatGPT's memory feature mixes contexts (e.g., running advice bleeding into product work). Zevi uses ChatGPT Projects and Claude Projects as "shared folders of chats" with custom instructions and knowledge bases for each domain. This keeps the AI in the right context and avoids contamination between different facets of work and life.^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-01-18-zevi-arnovitz.md]
