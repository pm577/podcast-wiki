# Nico Laqua

Co-Founder and CEO of Corgi Insurance, an AI-native insurance carrier built for startups. Raised $106M at a $2.6BN valuation within 2 years of founding. Known for creating one of the most intense workplace cultures in America — 7-day workweeks, founder sleeps in office, 24-hour Corgi Cafe, and 60% of first 30 employees have Corgi tattoos.

## Key Views

### 1. University Was a Massive Waste of Time

Nico argues that traditional university education is unnecessary for entrepreneurial success. His perspective is rooted in his own experience building a $2.6BN company without a conventional educational path, suggesting that real-world building and learning on the job is more valuable than formal education for founders.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-30-20vc-corgi-insurance-the-mos.md]

### 2. Why We Work Seven Days a Week

Nico is unapologetic about Corgi's extreme work culture. The philosophy: early-stage companies that move fastest win, and that requires total commitment from the founding team. The intensity is framed not as exploitation but as aligned mission-driven effort where every team member has volunteered for the pace.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-30-20vc-corgi-insurance-the-mos.md]

### 3. Work Trials Over Traditional Interviews

Corgi's hiring process uses extended work trials rather than traditional interviews. The model tests real skills and cultural fit over several days of paid work. This approach ensures that new hires understand the intensity of Corgi's culture before committing, and the company gets to evaluate actual output rather than interview performance.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-30-20vc-corgi-insurance-the-mos.md]

### 4. AI-Native Insurance: Building from Scratch in a Regulated Industry

Corgi Insurance was built as an AI-native company from day one, embedding AI into every aspect of the insurance value chain — from underwriting to claims to customer service. In a heavily regulated industry like insurance, being AI-native from inception gives Corgi a structural advantage over incumbents trying to retrofit AI onto legacy systems.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-30-20vc-corgi-insurance-the-mos.md]
