# Patrick Forquer

Chief Revenue Officer at Legora, the fastest growing enterprise business to ever hit $100M ARR (achieved in 18 months, on track for $250M+ ARR by year-end). Legora raised a $550M Series D at a $5.55B valuation led by Accel (20VC participated as an investor).

## Key Views

### 1. How Jude Law Generated $50 Million in Qualified Pipeline

Patrick shared the remarkable story of how actor Jude Law generated $50M in qualified sales pipeline for Legora. This highlights the power of strategic celebrity partnerships in enterprise AI — using recognizable figures to build credibility, open doors with C-suite buyers, and differentiate in a crowded market.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-11-20vc-inside-legora-100m-arr.md]

### 2. Why Implementation Is Your Secret Weapon to Win in AI

In enterprise AI sales, Patrick argues that implementation quality — not product features — is the primary competitive differentiator. Companies that invest in white-glove onboarding, customization, and ongoing support win deals even when competitors have technically superior products. In AI, where trust is low and switching costs feel high, implementation excellence is the ultimate moat.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-11-20vc-inside-legora-100m-arr.md]

### 3. Why Legora Is Undervalued at $5.5BN

Patrick makes the case that Legora's $5.5B valuation significantly undervalues the company given its trajectory ($100M ARR in 18 months, on track for $250M+). His argument: the enterprise AI market is in its earliest innings, and companies that achieve escape velocity in this environment will compound faster than historical SaaS benchmarks suggest.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-11-20vc-inside-legora-100m-arr.md]

### 4. Competing Against Harvey, the 800-Pound Gorilla

Patrick shared Legora's strategy for competing against Harvey, the dominant player in legal AI. His view: don't try to out-Harvey Harvey. Instead, find segments where incumbents are underserving customers, focus on implementation quality (see above), and build deep expertise in specific verticals where Legora can provide better outcomes.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2026-05-11-20vc-inside-legora-100m-arr.md]
