# Crystal Widjaja

*Chief Product Officer at Kumu. Former growth lead at Gojek (now GoTo), one of Southeast Asia's largest super apps with 170M+ users. Founded Generation Girl nonprofit for women in STEM.*

## Key Views

1. **Do things that don't scale to validate growth hypotheses**: At Gojek, Crystal rented a stadium and hired 60,000 drivers in a few weeks by pre-loading phones and running a job fair. To test a subscription feature, she added drivers to a WhatsApp group and had them manually sell to customers in person. Scrappy, unscalable experiments validated the model before any tech was built.

2. **Marketplaces are gardens, not construction sites**: Unlike SaaS (which is linear — build features, sell them), marketplaces are complex ecosystems where actions have delayed ripple effects. You must tread lightly because something you change today might cause chaos two months later. The gardener's touch — light, observant, iterative — is essential.

3. **Super apps thrive where phone storage is limited**: In Southeast Asia, many users have a phone as their only computer. With limited storage, users delete apps — so survival requires doing everything well enough in one app. This leapfrog dynamic (skipping desktop entirely) explains why super apps dominate in Asia but haven't emerged in the US.

4. **Follow the value prop, not the hype**: Crystal joined Gojek because the problem was viscerally clear: 2-hour traffic for 20km in Jakarta meant motorcycle taxis were an obvious solution. She took a Warren Buffett approach — understand the value prop, believe in the market, and be willing to take a bet even when the company was operating out of a house.

5. **The best career step is the one with little to lose**: Crystal cold-emailed Gojek from the US, moved to Indonesia with no safety net, and built data/fraud/growth teams from scratch. Her advice: when you're early in your career, take asymmetric bets where you have little to lose and everything to gain. She didn't need to know a "career path" — she just needed a problem that excited her.
