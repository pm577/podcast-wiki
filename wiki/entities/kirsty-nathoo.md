# Kirsty Nathoo

CFO of Y Combinator. The financial backbone of Y Combinator — controls and manages YC's internal finances, from paying bills to organizing demo days, wiring money to startups, and helping portfolio companies coordinate outside financings, tax issues, incorporation, and other fiscal matters.

## Key Views

### 1. The Financial Engine Behind Y Combinator

As CFO of YC, Kirsty holds "the keys to the kingdom" — she not only manages YC's finances but also helps thousands of portfolio companies navigate the complex fiscal landscape of startup operations. From incorporation to tax strategy to fundraising mechanics, her role is critical in ensuring YC companies can focus on building while the financial back-end is handled.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2015-09-28-20-vc-075-y-combinator-week.md]
