# Jessica Fain

Jessica Fain appeared on Lenny's Podcast to discuss the art of influence: the single most important skill ai can't replace.

## Key Views

**Influence is the Meta-Skill** — 'As product builders, there's almost not a skill that's more important. Influence and building momentum behind great ideas is the way great products actually get built. If you don't have buy-in from key stakeholders and executives, you can't build great products.' ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-03-22-jessica-fain.md]

**Execs Are Not Mind-Readers** — 'People completely misunderstand how executives make decisions.' An exec's calendar is 'a strobe light going off' — 8AM finance meeting, then interview, people problem, legal, product review. 'They have not had the time, energy, or wherewithal to center your problems.' Take 30 seconds to reset context: why we're here, what happened last time, why this matters. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-03-22-jessica-fain.md]

**It's Your Fault If They Don't Buy In** — 'You can't just say 'They just don't see it.' If they agreed to do something else, that's you not being able to influence them.' Own the communication failure. PMs have curiosity and empathy for users but forget those skills when talking to executives. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-03-22-jessica-fain.md]

**Kill Things to Build Trust** — 'One of the biggest things you can do to build trust is kill things, deprioritize things.' Showing you can kill your own projects proves you care about the company's success, not just your own agenda. 'You get paid to be a domain expert. Your executive is looking for you to be the deepest person in the room.' ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-03-22-jessica-fain.md]

**Communication Chameleons** — 'We as product leaders have to be communication chameleons. We have to speak their language — like love languages, but for work.' Does your exec turn on with a design, a customer story, a dashboard, or an experiment? Understand how their best brain gets activated. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/lenny-2026-03-22-jessica-fain.md]
