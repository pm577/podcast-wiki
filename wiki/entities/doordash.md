# DoorDash

Food delivery platform. Customer of Applied Compute. Onboards 100K+ merchants per year and uses AI for menu extraction — converting unstructured merchant data (images of menus) into structured DoorDash storefronts following their specific style guide.

^[raw/transcripts/stanford-ai-frontier/yash-bottla-applied-compute-stanford-ai-frontier.md]

## AI Use Case

General models couldn't handle DoorDash's specific style guide for modifiers, add-ons, and special ingredients. Applied Compute's approach: collect model outputs, humans correct, train against error rate.
