# Posterous

Dead simple blog platform founded by Garry Tan. Got into YC in 2008, raised ~$4M, hired 10 people. Sold to Twitter 3 years later for $20M. Used as a comparison point: 2 years of work with 10 people and $4M can now be done in 5 days with a $200/month Claude Code Max plan.
