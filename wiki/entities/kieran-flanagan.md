# Kieran Flanagan

SVP Marketing (later CMO) at HubSpot. Helped HubSpot grow internationally, transition to a product-led business, quadruple its marketing demand, and build out its media team including the acquisition of 'The Hustle.' Advisor and investor in early-stage companies.

## Key Views

### 1. You Need a Growth Hire Pre-Product-Market-Fit

Kieran argues that companies shouldn't wait until they hit product-market fit to hire for growth. The right growth hire early can accelerate the path to PMF by bringing quantitative rigor, experimentation discipline, and distribution thinking before the product is fully baked. Waiting too long means leaving growth potential on the table.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2022-08-10-20-growth-why-you-need-a-grow.md]

### 2. Every Company Will Be a Media Company

Kieran's thesis: every business, regardless of industry, must become a media company to survive. HubSpot's acquisition of 'The Hustle' exemplifies this — building owned media audiences creates direct distribution channels that bypass platform dependency. The companies that invest in content, communities, and owned channels will have a durable competitive advantage.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2022-08-10-20-growth-why-you-need-a-grow.md]

### 3. What Makes Communities Work (and Why Many Don't)

Kieran shared his framework for building successful communities: the best communities have clear purpose, active moderation, and genuine member investment. Many communities fail because they're started for vanity metrics rather than real member value. The key signal: are members helping each other without prompting?^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2022-08-10-20-growth-why-you-need-a-grow.md]
