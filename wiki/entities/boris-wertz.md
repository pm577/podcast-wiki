# Boris Wertz

Founding Partner of Version One Ventures, one of North America's leading early-stage funds. Portfolio includes Coinbase, AngelList, Shippo, TopHat, and Polychain Capital. Also a Board Partner at Andreessen Horowitz and lead independent director at Ether Capital. Previously COO of AbeBooks.com (sold to Amazon in 2008) where he led marketing, business development, product, and international operations. Named Pacific Ernst & Young Entrepreneur Of The Year in 2005.

## Key Views

### 1. The Ultimate Marketplace Playbook

Wertz provides a comprehensive framework for building and investing in marketplace businesses. Drawing on his experience at AbeBooks and as a board partner at a16z, he breaks down the dynamics of marketplace formation: how to solve the chicken-and-egg problem, the importance of fragmented supply, and why network effects determine long-term winners.

^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2015-12-28-20-vc-the-ultimate-guide-to-m.md]

### 2. Crypto as the Biggest Disruptor to VC

Wertz argues that crypto and decentralized platforms represent the biggest disruptive force to hit venture capital in a decade. He explains why investing in decentralized platforms requires a new mental model — one that accounts for protocol-level value capture, token incentives, and community-driven governance — and why traditional VC thinking doesn't apply.
