# Aaron Harris

Partner at Y Combinator. Cofounder of Tutorspree (Sequoia-backed, YC S11). Previously worked at Bridgewater Associates managing product and operations for an analytics group. Host of Startup School Radio.

## Key Views

### 1. YC's Partner Perspective on Early-Stage Investing

As a YC Partner, Aaron worked with startups from the earliest possible stage. His background spans both founder and investor sides — having built a Sequoia-backed company and worked at Bridgewater Associates, one of the world's largest hedge funds, giving him a unique lens on startup operations and analytical decision-making.^[raw/transcripts/20vc/20vc-2015-09-30-20-vc-076-y-combinator-week.md]
