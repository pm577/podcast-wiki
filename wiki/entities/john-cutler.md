# John Cutler

John Cutler is a product management thought leader and researcher known for his frameworks on software adoption, product sense, and organizational dynamics. He is referenced by [[Elena Verna]] in discussions about the stages of AI product adoption.

## Key Frameworks

### Capability → Value → Scale

Software adoption passes through three stages:
1. **Capability** — what's possible (vibe coding's current stage)
2. **Value** — how users derive benefit
3. **Scale** — embed into workflows

Because [[AI]] products change every 1-3 months, users constantly re-explore — creating a natural re-engagement loop that traditional software doesn't have.

### Product Sense

The ability to develop intuition about what users need before they can articulate it. This is especially critical in fast-moving categories where traditional user research can't keep up.
