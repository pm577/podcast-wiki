# Meltem Kuran Berkowitz

Meltem Kuran Berkowitz is Head of Growth at Deel, the fastest growing SaaS business of all time ($0 to $300M ARR in 3 years, EBITDA positive). She joined Deel as employee ~20 and leads all growth efforts including paid ads, product marketing, content, community, brand, and more.

## Key Views

**Build the Skeleton Before You Put on the Makeup** — Early stage growth advice: start with the basics. Do you have a fast website? Can search engines find it? Can people find it? Only after those are answered should you consider paid ads. "You can't run a successful paid ads program if you have a website that's loading in four plus seconds." Go back to the basics, build a good core experience, then expand step by step. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/meltem-kuran.md]

**Low-Cost Growth Channels First** — Deel kickstarted growth through low-cost channels: tapping into communities like Reddit, content marketing, and SEO. Before spending money on paid acquisition, exhaust organic channels that can provide compounding returns. Content and SEO were particularly effective for Deel's global audience. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/meltem-kuran.md]

**Evolving Growth Investment as the Company Scales** — The growth playbook changes dramatically at different revenue stages: at $1M ARR, focus on finding channels; at $50M ARR, optimize and scale proven channels; at $300M ARR, build a full growth team with specialized functions. Each stage requires different team structures, metrics, and investment levels. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/meltem-kuran.md]

**Culture of Speed and Optimism** — Deel's extraordinary growth was fueled by a culture that values speed and optimism. Decisions are made quickly, experiments are run constantly, and the team maintains a bias toward action. This culture is something that must be intentionally built and protected as the company scales. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/meltem-kuran.md]

**Structuring an Early Growth Team** — Early growth teams should be generalists who can work across channels. Only as the company grows should you hire specialists in SEO, paid ads, content, product marketing, etc. The first growth person needs to be comfortable doing everything and building processes from scratch. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/meltem-kuran.md]
