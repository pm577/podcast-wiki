# Adam Fishman

Adam Fishman is a growth and product leader who has worked at Patreon and Lyft. He has appeared on [[Lenny's Podcast]] discussing growth team structure, experimentation, and how growth roles evolve at different company stages.

## Key Frameworks

### Growth Team Evolution

Adam's framework for how growth teams should evolve as companies scale:
- **Early stage:** Growth is everyone's job — no dedicated team
- **Growth stage:** Dedicated growth team focused on experimentation
- **Scale stage:** Growth becomes specialized (acquisition, activation, retention)

### Growth Team Structure

Different org structures for different contexts:
- **Vertical:** Growth team owns a full vertical (e.g., self-serve growth)
- **Horizontal:** Growth team provides tools and experimentation infrastructure
- **Hybrid:** Mix of vertical ownership and horizontal enablement
