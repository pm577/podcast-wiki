# Melissa (Tan)

**Note:** This entity page covers the content in the `melissa.md` transcript, which features Melissa Perri (not Melissa Tan despite the title). The transcript title says "Melissa Tan" but the content speaker is "Melissa Perri." See also the dedicated [[melissa-perri]] and [[melissa-tan]] pages.

Melissa Perri is a legend in the product management community and author of *Escaping the Build Trap*.

## Key Views

**Most Issues Are in Strategy Deployment, Not Training** — "I've met a lot of organizations that think most of their issues are in the training of their people. 99% of the time, it's actually in the way they're setting their goals and deploying their strategy." Once you train people but have no context for them to work toward, the training is useless. Transformations need a holistic approach. ^[raw/transcripts/lenny/melissa.md]

**Build Trap** — Product teams often measure success by output (features shipped) rather than outcomes (customer value delivered). Escaping the build trap requires shifting to outcome-driven product development.

**Product Ops Enables PMs** — Product operations creates shared systems that allow PMs to focus on strategic work instead of operational overhead.
