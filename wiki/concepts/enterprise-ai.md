# Enterprise AI

The application of AI models to enterprise-specific tasks and workflows. Unlike general-purpose AI, enterprise AI requires:

1. **Specialization** — general models set the floor, specialization sets the ceiling
2. **Proprietary data** — enterprises have the most valuable data; it needs to be leveraged for training
3. **Custom evals** — each enterprise has its own definition of what good and bad looks like
4. **The "model + harness + context" trinity** — application-layer innovation matters as much as the model

## Key Principle

"Smart geniuses that know nothing about your business." Frontier models are powerful generalists, but they lack enterprise-specific knowledge. The most valuable AI is trained on proprietary enterprise data.
