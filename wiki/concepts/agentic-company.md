# The Agentic Company

An organizational model where AI agents are embedded into every decision-making process. Company operates as a closed-loop system with tight feedback, minimal lossy information, and self-healing capabilities.

## How Primitives Map to Company Structure

| Primitive | Org Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Skill | Employee with a capability |
| Resolver | Org chart (who handles what) |
| Filing rules / brain | Internal process |
| Check resolvable | Audit & compliance |
| Trigger eval | Performance review |

## Three Roles

1. **IC (Individual Contributor)** — everyone ships. Non-technical people can build with AI tools.
2. **DRI (Directly Responsible Individual)** — owns every outcome. Orchestrates ICs. Often the founder.
3. **AI Founder** — lives at the edge of the future, tries all tools, brings innovations into the company.

## Metrics

- 1 employee → $1-2M revenue (vs public SaaS under $100K)
- Sprint time cut in half, 10x output (YC engineering team)
- Companies 3x-ing in 3 months (YC average)
