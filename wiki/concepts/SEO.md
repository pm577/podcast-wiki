# SEO

## Synthesis

1. **SEO is being fundamentally disrupted by AI-driven search.** Eli Schwartz, SEO advisor and author, argues that the rise of AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and LLM-powered search changes the SEO playbook. Rankings are no longer about keywords — they're about being the source that AI models trust and cite. Companies need to optimize for "Answer Engine Optimization" (AEO) rather than traditional keyword density.

2. **Technical SEO still matters, but differently.** Schwartz notes that for small SaaS websites (under 100 pages), technical SEO is straightforward — proper sitemaps, clean URLs, fast load times. The real technical SEO challenges only appear at "Zillow scale" (millions of pages). Most SaaS companies should focus on content quality and authority rather than technical edge cases.

3. **AI is making search results zero-click — which changes the value of traffic.** Robby Stein (VP Product, Google Search) explains that Google's AI Mode and AI Overviews aim to answer queries directly in search results. This reduces click-through rates to websites. The adaptation: create content that builds brand awareness even in zero-click scenarios, and diversify traffic sources beyond organic search.

4. **E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) is becoming the ranking framework for AI search.** Ethan Smith (Graphite) describes how to optimize for ChatGPT recommendations — you need actual expertise demonstrated through original research, cited data, and author credentials. Generic content optimized for keyword density is being aggressively filtered out by AI search tools.

5. **The SEO timeline is compressing.** Simon Willison notes that SEO used to be a 6-12 month play. With AI, content can surface in search results within days if it addresses a genuinely novel query. The winners are moving fast on emerging topics and building topical authority through interconnected content clusters.

## Key Disagreements

- **SEO is dead vs. SEO is evolving:** Some guests argue AI search kills SEO; others say it raises the bar for quality.
- **Programmatic SEO vs. manual content:** Debate over whether automated content generation at scale still works post-AI.
- **Link building value:** Some maintain backlinks remain the #1 ranking signal; others argue content relevance and authority matter more.
