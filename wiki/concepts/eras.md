# AI/Tech Eras

The 20VC podcast spans multiple distinct eras in the technology industry. Each era reflects the dominant investment thesis, founder sentiment, and macro environment of its time.

---

## Era Overview


- **[Post-Financial Crisis Recovery](era-post-financial-crisis-recovery)** (2015-01 to 2016-06) — 206 episodes (14.1%)
  - The early years of The Twenty Minute VC podcast coincided with a period of steady recovery from the 2008 financial crisis.
- **[SaaS Scaling Era](era-saas-scaling-era)** (2016-07 to 2018-12) — 326 episodes (22.3%)
  - By mid-2016 the podcast shifted focus toward the SaaS boom.
- **[Late-Stage Boom](era-late-stage-boom)** (2019-01 to 2021-06) — 113 episodes (7.7%)
  - The late 2010s saw an explosion of mega-rounds,独角兽 (unicorn) companies, and record-breaking fund sizes.
- **[COVID Era](era-covid-era)** (2020-03 to 2021-12) — 176 episodes (12.0%)
  - The COVID-19 pandemic triggered a dramatic shift in how startups operated and how VCs invested.
- **[Correction & Efficiency](era-correction-and-efficiency)** (2022-01 to 2023-06) — 213 episodes (14.6%)
  - Beginning in 2022, the macro environment shifted sharply.
- **[AI Inflection](era-ai-inflection)** (2023-07 to 2026-12) — 428 episodes (29.3%)
  - The public release of ChatGPT in late 2022, followed by GPT-4 in March 2023, triggered a seismic shift in the tech landscape.

---

*Total: 1462 episodes across 6 active eras.*
