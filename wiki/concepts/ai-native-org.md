# AI-Native Organization

A company designed from first principles to operate with AI agents as core infrastructure — not bolted on after the fact.

## Key Characteristics

1. **Closed-loop decision making** — agents have read access to every artifact (GitHub, Discord, meetings)
2. **Lossless information** — no unwritten meeting notes, no side conversations without trace
3. **Self-healing** — agents suggest next items, surface bugs, improve over time
4. **Flat structure** — less need for middle management (historically existed for lossy information routing)
5. **DRI model** — every outcome has one direct responsible individual

## Before vs After

Before: open loop, lossy, error accumulates, side conversations, vibes
After: closed loop, tight feedback, self-healing, all artifacts connected
