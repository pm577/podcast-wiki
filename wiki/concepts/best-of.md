# Best Of

## Synthesis

1. **"Best of" compilations serve as evergreen content anchors.** Podcast guests consistently note that "best of" episodes — whether best advice, best frameworks, or best stories — consistently outperform regular episodes in long-term engagement. They serve as entry points for new listeners and reference material for existing audiences.

2. **Curated content demonstrates editorial judgment.** The value of a "best of" is not just in the content but in the curation itself. Albert Cheng emphasizes that curation shows deep domain expertise — knowing what matters enough to highlight it. The best compilations are not aggregations but arguments about what's most important.

3. **Themed compilations perform better than generic "greatest hits."** Rather than "best episodes of 2025," the most effective "best of" content clusters around themes: "best advice on fundraising," "best frameworks for product strategy," "best stories of failure and recovery." Theme-based curation helps niche audiences find relevant wisdom.

4. **"Best of" creates social proof for new audiences.** Ben Horowitz (a16z) discusses how people who discover companies through third-party validation often start with "best of" or "highlights" before diving into the full body of work. These compilations serve as cultural onboarding — letting new audiences understand the ethos quickly.

5. **Timeliness matters — "best of" should feel current.** Stewart Butterfield notes that even curated compilations need a freshness element. The best approach is to update "best of" lists periodically, adding new insights and removing outdated ones. A static "best of" list loses relevance as the industry evolves.

## Key Disagreements

- **Algorithmic vs. human curation:** Whether AI-driven recommendations or human editorial judgment creates better "best of" content.
- **Frequency:** Some recommend annual "best of" content; others produce quarterly or monthly highlights.
- **Format:** Whether "best of" works best as articles, episodes, playlists, or visual summaries.
