# Building in Public

Building in public is the practice of openly sharing a company's development journey — product updates, failures, learnings, and decisions — as a primary growth and community-building strategy. For AI-native companies, it has become the #1 growth channel.

## Core Principles

### Ship → Announce → Repeat

The core rhythm: ship a marketable feature every week, then announce it publicly. This creates:
- **Constant market noise** — always something to talk about
- **Natural re-engagement** — users check what's new
- **User feedback loop** — short idea-to-ship cycles make users feel heard

### Founder-Led Socials

From [[Elena Verna]] and [[Anton Osika]] (Lovable): the CEO being active on X and LinkedIn creates authentic marketing that no corporate account can match. Combined with employee socials, every team member becomes a marketer.

### Vulnerability as Strategy

Authenticity and vulnerability outperform corporate scrubbing. Sharing failures and learnings builds trust that polished marketing never can.
